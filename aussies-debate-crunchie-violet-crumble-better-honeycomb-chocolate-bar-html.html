Aussies debate over whether the Crunchie or Violet Crumble is the better honeycomb chocolate bar

 Posted on June 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  464 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A fiery debate has erupted online over which Australian honeycomb chocolate bar is better: the Crunchie or the Violet Crumble.

Kathryn Skidmore ignited the discussion when she asked on Twitter what was prefered Crunchies or Violet Crumbles to resolve a debate with a friend.

Both the chocolate bars feature a crunchy honeycomb interior while the outside is covered in chocolate.
