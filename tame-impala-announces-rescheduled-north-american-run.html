Tame Impala Announces Rescheduled North American Run

 Posted on April 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  321 words  |  Aldo Pusey

(CelebrityAccess) — Kevin Parker, AKA Tame Impala, announced the rescheduled dates for his prevuiously postponed North American tour.

Parker will kick off the tour in at Foro Sol Stadium in Mexico City on July 22nd, 2021, and wrap on October 18th at American Airlines Arena in Miami, Florida.

Key plays on the tour include The Gorge in Washington, United Center in Chicago; and The Chase Center in San Fran.

As well, Tame Impala is lined up for several festivals in 2021, including Outside Lands in August and the Life is Beautiful festival in September.

Tame Impala will be making up dates that were originally planned for the summer of 2020, but were postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic.

Parker is touring in support of his fourth studio album Slow Rush, which dropped in February.
