Nobody wears a gorgeous gown quite like Nina Dobrev.

And with prom season coming to Mystic Falls, her "Vampire Diaries" character Elena Gilbert is definitely dressed to impress.

In new photos from the upcoming "Pictures of You" episode, we get our first look at her stunning dress ... and wow, she looks incredible.

We're loving the cut and color of the gown, which also matches her bright new hair extensions.

But you might have to think twice about buying this dress for your own school dance. The Theia Printed Sweetheart Gown goes for $1495 over at Neiman Marcus ... and is sold out.
