Selena Gomez Wore a Ruby-Red Silk Gown at the 2024 Golden Globes

 Posted on March 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Selena Gomez spends her time solving mysteries on the hit Hulu series Only Murders in the Building, but it’s no mystery that she absolutely stunned on the Golden Globes red carpet.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

For the first major event of award season, Gomez wore a custom Giorgio Armani Privé ruby-red silk gown, with a high-neck halter top with cutouts featuring a pleated detail and three black crystal-embroidered flowers going down the bodice. The look was completed with a matching asymmetrical full skirt with a black waistband, as well as ruby pointed-toe heels and sparkling silver jewelry on both wrists.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

The nominee also carried the JW PEI Gabbi bag, which has been seen on the arms of such celebs as Gigi Hadid, Hailey Bieber, Irina Shayk, and more. Gomez sported the bag in a red crystal color, which perfectly matched the rest of her ensemble.

Matt Winkelmeyer//Getty Images

Tonight, the 31-year-old star is nominated in the category Best Female Actor in a Television Series – Musical or Comedy, thanks to her humorous performance as Mabel Mora in Only Murders in the Building. This is the second year in a row Gomez has been nominated for the role, and her co-leads Steve Martin and Martin Short are both also nominated for Best Male Actor.

Gomez has really made red her signature color in the past few months. As you might recall, the superstar wore a similar crimson shade when she appeared at the 2023 Video Music Awards in September. For that event, she sported a beaded Oscar de la Renta dress that featured flower-shaped appliqués, which fell into a fringe train, as well as a plunging balconette bra neckline and high leg slit.
