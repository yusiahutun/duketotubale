Zendaya fears surface as she unfollows everyone - including Tom Holland

 Posted on July 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  423 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Euphoria star Zendaya has unfollowed everyone on Instagram, including Tom Holland.

The actress has removed everyone from her following list on Instagram and now follows no one. Not even her boyfriend of two years, Tom Holland. People are questioning the move as she has still been active on both the photo-sharing app and X (formerly Twitter), though sparsely, throughout the last few months.

Zendaya’s strange move on Instagram and Tom Holland isn’t given special treatment

The TV star now follows no one, despite still using her account. She only posted a few days to promote one of her upcoming projects, Challengers, which also features her Dune movie co-star Timothée Chalamet.

On January 2, she shared the funky poster for the film, which sees the Hollywood actress sporting some shades that mirror the reflections of both Timothée and their other co-star Josh O’Connor.

She captioned the post: “Challengers April 26th. Wishing you all the most beautiful new year [sparkles emoji].”

Fans question Zendaya’s move

After she unfollowed Tom Holland on Instagram, fans took to X to question her move.

Someone else penned: “Not Zendaya unfollowing almost everyone on Instagram?!?!? [two crying face emojis].”

Another said: “Zendaya unfollowing everyone on Instagram oh she thinks she Beyoncé now

A third wrote: “What’s going on with Zendaya that has unfollowed people? Even Tom, I don’t understand nothing… I think that in any moment she will delete her Instagram account [crying face and heartbroken emoji].”

One Zendaya fan suggested it had to “be a glitch” as “she would never unfollow Beyoncé.”

There has been no indication as to why Zendaya has unfollowed people, including Tom, and she hasn’t released a statement as to why, either.

Tom and Zendaya engagement rumors

Even though the 27-year-old unfollowed her beau Tom Holland on Instagram, we can assume things are still going well as there were recent rumors about them getting ready to tie the knot … before Zendaya shut them down.

In a now-deleted clip, the Spider-Man: Homecoming actress said the large pearl ring, which was slotted onto her left hand in a snap she’d posted was not an engagement one.

She said: “I can’t post anything, you guys. I posted it for my hat. Like not for the ring on my right hand, you guys, seriously.

“You think that’s how I would drop the news? You think, like, what.”
