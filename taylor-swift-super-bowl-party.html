Providing food for a Super Bowl party is no easy feat. If cooking isn’t your forte, don’t sweat it; there are some amazing online food delivery services that can step in.

Goldbelly

Goldbelly is an online marketplace for food products from restaurants, bakeries, delis, and world-renowned chefs. Some come ready to eat, while others require a bit of prep and cooking.

Omaha Steaks

You’ll never run out of meat, seafood, sides, desserts, and appetizers to add to your Omaha Steaks cart. Similar to Goldbelly, some of the foods involve prep and cooking, while others simply need to be popped into the oven or onto the stove and voila!

ButcherBox

ButcherBox offers 100 percent grass-fed, grass-finished beef, free-range organic chicken, pork raised crate-free, and wild-caught seafood. Are you drooling yet?

HelloFresh

While you might be wondering why a meal-kit company is included, HelloFresh has a multitude of Super Bowl-ready recipes. You’ll receive a box with all of the ingredients neatly portioned out and packaged. All you have to do is follow the recipe card instructions and you’ll be feasting in no time.
