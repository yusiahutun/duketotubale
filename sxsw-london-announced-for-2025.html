SXSW London Announced For 2025

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  244 words  |  Larita Shotwell

LONDON (CelebrityAccess) — On Wednesday, organizers for the annual South By Southwest conference for music and film, announced plans to expand across the Atlantic next year with SXSW London.

According to event organizers, the London expansion will develop its own personality, based on the city’s established cultural life and proximity to Europe’s creative and tech centers.

The event will take place in June 2025 and SXSW London will take over dozens of venues, galleries, clubs, and other spaces in Shoreditch, from programming across music, technology, and entertainment.

Founded in Austin in 1987, SXSW has grown to become a cultural touchstone for the music, entertainment and tech worlds. The SXSW expanded into the South Pacific with the launch of the brand in Sydney in 2023.

Additional details about the London event, including a programming lineup, will be announced in the coming months, organizers said.

“I am delighted to welcome SXSW to London for the first time ever – confirming our place at the heart of Europe’s tech and creative sectors and as a global capital of culture. When I was part of SXSW in Austin in 2018, I saw first-hand the electric atmosphere of innovation SXSW creates and I can’t wait to be part of it again. This is a historic opportunity for London to once again bring the world’s most exciting talent together as part of our mission to build a better and more prosperous London for everyone,” stated London Mayor Sadiq Khan.
