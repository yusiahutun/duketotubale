Pia Mia is a massive pop star in the making.

She first broke out in the music scene at the age of 14 when her 2011 Bubblegum Boy released. The young singer then followed it up with the song, Do It Again, with high profile musical artists like Chris Brown and Tyga in 2015.

Besides releasing studio music, she is also quite active on Youtube. Her cover of Drake's Hold On, We’re Going Home also grabbed major eyeball from music lovers.

Boyfriend History

Although quite young, Pia already has a fair share of romantic escapades in her life.

Being an avid social media user, she mentioned having two affairs till date on 9th December 2012. Well, one of the said boyfriends was Remy Thorne - brother of Disney alum Bella Thorne. The pair were close; Pia posted multiple declarations of love for her beau via Twitter.

The dating relationship brought joy in Pia’s life as it was her collaboration with Remy's sister Bella in Bubblegum Boy song, which led to her breakout in the music industry. However, teenage love didn't last, and the pair parted ways. Post the break-up; fans blamed Pia that she used Remy’s famous sister to climb the ladders of fame.

The blame game didn’t stop Pia from enjoying her dating life. She started dating music producer-director Nic Nac in 2014. However, the pair’s romance hit a roadblock, and they parted ways after two years in 2016.

After the heartbreak, The Gift hitmaker's name got linked with G-Eazy in 2016. The conjectures began when the musicians were spotted getting close while heading to a club, post the MTV Video Music Awards.

Family Background

Pia was born in a small island of US, Guam. Her life at her native place was slow paced with lots of family-time alongside barbeques. Pia's mother homeschooled her. Thus, she found a bundle of time to post cover songs on YouTube.

At a mere age of 13, Pia managed to convince her mom to move to L.A. to pursue her musical career. The leap of faith worked out as she has released two successful EPs, The Gift and The Gift 2. On 26th April 2019, she released the song titled Bitter Love, which got a lot of love from the audience.

Pia Mia in a still from her single Bitter Love, released on 26th April 2019 ( Photo: Pia Mia's Instagram)

Well, her two YouTube channels, PiaMia and PrincessPiaMiaVEVO, fetch her a decent revenue. The former brings an average of $359 - $5.7K annually while the latter adds an average of $20K - $319.7K per year to her net worth.

Pia also added working in the fashion industry to her bio. She launched an exclusive collection titled PiaMia with UK fashion magnate, In The Style, in 2017. Courtesy of the emerging career, she is increasing her net worth day by day.

Taking about Pia’s measurements, she stands at the height of 5 feet 5 inches (1.65 m) and boasts a weight of 48 kgs. Well, she also boasts European and Oceanian ethnicity.
