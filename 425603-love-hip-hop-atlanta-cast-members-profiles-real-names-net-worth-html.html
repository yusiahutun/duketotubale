Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta cast profiles

Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta has seen many characters come and go. However, the main characters have remained with the show since the first season. Here is a look at the ten cast members who have appeared in at least 80 episodes.

1. Karlie Redd

Karlie Redd is an American television personality, rapper, model, and actress. She was born Karlie Lewis on 15 April 1974 (age 47) in The Bronx, New York City, New York, United States. Karlie has been the starring of the show since the first season. But before the show, she appeared in other television shows, including One on One (2003) and Scream Queens (2010).

Apart from reality TV, Karlie Redd is a businesswoman. In 2012, she launched the Redd Remy Hairline and later opened a women's clothing store called Merci Boutique in 2015. As of 2021, Karlie Redd has a net worth of $1.2 million.

2. Rasheeda

Rasheeda started her career as a musician in 1998. She was part of a female hip-hop trio called Da Kaperz. In 2000, she ditched the group to embark on a solo career and released her first album, Dirty South, in 2001. She has released several albums since 2001, including A Ghetto Dream (2002), GA Peach (2006), Dat Type of Gurl (2007), Certified Hot Chick (2009), and Boss Chick Music (2012).

What is Rasheeda's nationality? Rasheeda was born Rasheeda Buckner-Frost on 25 May 1982 in Illinois. That makes her nationality American. Rasheeda first joined Love & Hip-Hop: Atlanta in 2012. She has appeared in all ten seasons of the show, totalling 159 episodes. As of 2021, Rasheeda has a net worth of $600,000.

3. Mimi Faust

Mimi Faust was born Oluremi Fela James Faust on 3 January 1970 (age 51) in Newport News, Virginia, United States. She is a reality television personality who has been a main cast member on Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta since 2012. The show introduced her when she was in an on-again, off-again relationship with fellow Stevie J.

4. Stevie J

Stevie J is an American DJ, record producer, and television personality. He was born Steven Aaron Jordan on 2 November 1971 (age 49) in Utica, New York, United States. 5. Kirk Frost

Kirk Frost is an American reality television star and entertainment manager. Are Rasheeda and Kirk still married? Kirk Frost married Rasheeda in 1999 when she was 23 years old. The couple has two sons, born in 2000 and 2013. As of 2021, Rasheeda and Kirk are still married.
