If you are a fan of the police drama series, then New York Undercover is the show for you. First airing on Fox in September 1994, the show grew to become a classic success. But as time has passed by, many fans are curious to know about the cast's whereabouts. So, where are the cast of New York Undercover?

TABLE OF CONTENTS

New York Undercover is an American police drama that aired for four seasons on the Fox television network from 8 September 1994 to 11 February 1999. The T.V. show was the first police drama on American television to feature two people of colour in the starring roles.

The cast of New York Undercover

The cast of New York Undercover comprises a diverse group of talented actors who brought the show to life. The series portrayed undercover police work, with the cast performing the roles well. Some of the cast members have reprised their roles in the New York Undercover reboot.

1. Malik Yoba (seasons 1–4)

  • Full name: Abdul-Malik Kashie Yoba

Abdul-Malik Kashie Yoba is an American actor. He plays J.C. Williams, a young undercover detective in New York City's Fourth Precinct during the show's original run and reboot. 2. Michael DeLorenzo (seasons 1–3)
