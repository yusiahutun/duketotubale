Paris Hilton has shared more photos from her epic 43rd birthday getaway to Big Sky, Montana.

The photos captured Hilton relaxing at the Yellowstone Club, a private ski and golf community, with her son Phoenix as she took in the gorgeous mountain views. 

Her one-year-old son, whom she shares with husband Carter Reum, donned an adorable Moschino bear look and black beanie as he sat in the arms of his mother.

Paris - who also has daughter London with Carter - sported a chic black-and-white jacket, beanie, and sunglasses as she admired the snowy scenery.

The post also contained an array of other birthday snaps, along with a glimpse into her lavish celebrations on her private jet. 'Best Birthday Weekend Ever!' she captioned the post.

The airplane bash included a lavish display of balloons greeting Paris at the entrance, champagne, and plenty of sweet treats.

She included a video of Carter kissing her as they posed in the snow and a clip of her blowing out the candles to her pretty pink birthday cake.

The post also contained other family snaps, whether she was playing with her son in his high chair or reading to him on board their jet.  

The socialite celebrated her 43rd birthday on Saturday with a post highlighting memorable moments in her life over the past year.

'Another sparkling year of pure #Sliving! Feeling so blessed for every lesson this year has gifted me. From motherhood to wifey duties, girl boss moments, and standing up for what’s right… This has been the most iconic year yet! #Aquarius #SlivingBirthday,' she captioned the post.

Her photos ranged from glam moments to family moments to business moments and celebrating each part of her life from the past year.

Hilton and Reum, who began dating in 2019 before tying the knot in 2021, now have two children together. 

They started their family with the arrival of Phoenix, who was born via surrogate in January of 2023.

In November 2023, they announced they had welcomed another child via surrogate, daughter London. London did not appear to be pictured in her latest birthday post. 

The social media figure opened up about motherhood during an interview with Romper, where she revealed that she was pleased to have such calm children.

'I feel so lucky because all my other friends who have kids are like, "I'm up all night. They're crying all night." My babies, they're just so calm, so chill,' she said. 

Hilton also spoke about why she chose to keep the news about her children withheld from the public until their respective arrivals.

'My life has been so public, so out there. I didn't want my son coming into this world with any negative energy,' she said.

The reality television personality then stated that she was pleased to have been able to control the narrative around her kids' arrivals.

'I'm really happy I did it that way, just for Carter and I to have that journey together without the outside world chirping in,' she said.
