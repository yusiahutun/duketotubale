Potato Peeling Trick - Easy Potato Idea

 Posted on February 28, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  184 words  |  Martina Birk

Peeling potatoes is a tricky business—even if you're not a perfectionist who likes to make sure that every measly scrap of skin is scraped and peeled away, it's a time-consuming task that can make potato-centered recipes that much harder to accomplish. Thankfully, the food experts over at Chow have brought back a brilliant old-school trick to turn this annoying task into an utterly effortless feat in just a few easy steps.

1. Boil your potatoes until they're fully cooked.

2. Place them in a bowl of ice water for 5-10 seconds, or until they've cooled down enough that you can pick them up.

3. Simply peel away the skin.
