Relive Carrie Underwood's Most Beautiful Pregnancy Pictures

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  263 words  |  Martina Birk

Carrie Underwood is pregnant with baby No. 2, and while we don't know when she's due, we do know that she'll show her baby bump off with style. Pictures from her first pregnancy prove the "Cry Pretty" singer doesn't abandon her commitment to fashion just because she's expecting.

Underwood announced she and husband Mike Fisher were pregnant with Isaiah on Labor Day 2014, and one week later she was working a red carpet. Photos taken in November and December of that year give the most to this gallery. The 2014 CMA Awards, CMA Country Christmas special and American Country Countdown Awards (remember those!?) featured a smiling Underwood performing and working the red carpet with class and style. She was definitely showing by mid-December.

After that, she fell off the radar until after baby Isaiah was born in February 2015. Will the same thing happen this time around? The 35-year-old's pregnancy news means she's most likely due in late 2018 or early 2019 — enough time to recover before her Cry Pretty Tour 360 begins on May 1. If you've got Jan. 27 in your Carrie Underwood due date office pool you should be feeling pretty good.

The Cry Pretty album is due to drop on Sept. 14. Underwood begins a new season as singer of the Sunday Night Football theme song very soon, and she'll return to host the CMA Awards for an 11th straight year on Nov. 14. There will be no shortage of opportunities for new pregnant Carrie Underwood pictures.
