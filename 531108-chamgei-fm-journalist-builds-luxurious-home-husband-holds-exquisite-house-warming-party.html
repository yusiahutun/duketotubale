Meru man builds mum posh house

In a different story, a Meru man built a new house for his mother with his first salary as a teacher.

Teacher Safari Bornface Tuluba did what many sons wished, building a new house for their mothers.

He did so with his first salary, savings, and a few loans from friends, a project that lasted two years since groundbreaking.

Safari explained that he prioritised his mother's house over his own to thank her for enduring numerous challenges to ensure he was educated at university.

Building mum house in Kakamega

Similarly, a former househelp built a house for her mum months after unveiling an impressive bungalow.

Deborah Odari worked as a house help before transitioning into a teacher and a county government employee.

Odari inspired many Kenyans after unveiling an impressive house she built in Kakamega county.

She revamped her mother's mud hose in Kitale and spoke to TUKO.co.ke about how she funded the project.
