The 15 Best Lip Glosses for Women 2024

Lancome Juicy Tubes

Lancome Juicy Tubes

There is no lip gloss more synonymous with the 2000s than Lancôme's inimitable Juicy Tubes. Riding the nostalgia wave, the brand re-released this fruity scented gloss last year, and we're happy to report it still delivers that mini dose of dopamine with every application.

Dior Addict Lip Maximizer

Dior Addict Lip Maximizer

Tiktok's current darling, Dior's Lip Maximiser is truly deserving of its hefty fandom. Available in a whole menu of shades, finishes and textures (the holographic finishes are surprisingly wearable), there's a nourishing, plumping pick here for just-about every beauty predilection.

Byredo Liquid Lipstick Vinyl

Byredo Liquid Lipstick Vinyl

Created by colour mastermind Lucia Pica, Byredo's high-pigment lip vinyls come in unusual yet immensely flattering shades. For major colour payoff and a latex-like shine, stop here.

Hermès Hermèsistible Oil

Hermès Hermèsistible Oil

Hermès leans into its playful side with these vibrant lip oils. Each one features a nostalgic fruity scent (our favourite is the Seville citrus of Corail Bigarade) and a wash of skin-brightening colour alongside plenty of plant oils to leave lips plumped and protected.

Victoria Beckham Beauty Posh Gloss

Victoria Beckham Beauty Posh Gloss

Victoria Beckham's Posh Gloss really is a triumphant formula: it's incredibly shiny, so delivers that wet-look gleam to major effect, and comes in a host of hues and finishes. We'd recommend the golden, glimmering Martini to set off a (faux) glow.

MAC Lipglass

MAC Lipglass

A favorite of makeup artists everywhere, there is no clear gloss better than MAC's Lipglass. It was designed to be worn alone or over your favourite lipstick, so it's not in the business of slipping and sliding around.

Tower 28 Beauty ShineOn Milky Lip Jelly

Tower 28 Beauty ShineOn Milky Lip Jelly

Now 20% Off

From brilliantly gentle make-up brand Tower 28 (a lifeline for those with eczema, rosacea and extreme sensitivities), this milky gloss combines a wash of soft colour with a flattering shine.

Clarins Lip Comfort Oil

Clarins Lip Comfort Oil

Another lip product with a hyper-loyal fanbase, Clarins' tinted tubes blend the hydration of a lip oil with the shine of a gloss. The best part? Absolutely no stickiness.

Westman Atelier Squeaky Clean Liquid Lip Balm

Westman Atelier Squeaky Clean Liquid Lip Balm

For a balm that comes with a bonus dose of colour, look to Westman Atelier's Squeaky Clean lip treatments. They combine a jelly-like sheen with plenty of plant oils to condition and cosset dry lips.

Brilliant Gloss

Brilliant Gloss

Chantecaille's lightweight lip gloss imparts a moderate shine and pigment, so is ideal for anyone who simply wants a subtle enhancement.
