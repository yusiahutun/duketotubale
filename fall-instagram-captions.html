150 Best Fall Captions for Instagram 2023

 Posted on May 22, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1281 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The weather is turning cool and crisp, and it's time to pull out your sweaters and order up some pumpkin spice lattes. With all those leaves falling in photogenic yellows and oranges and reds, there are plenty of autumn photo ops you'll want to document, from frame-worthy scenery pics to snaps of you and your friends enjoying fall activities (hay ride anyone?). But what to say? Not to fear. Here, you'll find 150 fall captions for Instagram, whether you're sharing a photos with friends, your significant other, or your dog!

Scroll on down to find just the right words to capture that fall vibe in your photo captions, from fall puns to song lyrics to fun seasonal sayings and quotes. Some are funny, some are cute, some are filled with emojis. All are pretty simple, with lots of options that cleverly nod to the falling leaves, be-leaf us.

Looking for more inspiration for your words? Check out these memorable fall quotes, these Halloween Instagram captions (that will make you laugh), and these Thanksgiving Instagram captions begging to be used when you eat all that turkey. Now time to read through these fall captions and pick the right one(s) for your pics!
