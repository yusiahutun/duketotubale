Halle Berry Posts Naked Photo on Instagram

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  1 minutes  |  163 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Halle Berry only joined Instagram a year ago, but she's already a pro at the app. She's got the quintessential images down: the bare-faced selfie, the throwback photo, the skyline shot and the bikini-clad beach pic. With her latest post, Berry tackles another Instagram staple: the naked selfie.

Yesterday, the Oscar-winning actress, 50, shared a nude (or so we think) photo of herself. Berry is pressed against a sheet of striped glass, showing off her silhouette. But if you look closely, it appears she might be wearing bikini bottoms. Swimsuit-clad or not, the overall result is a steamy optical illusion.

This isn't the first time the actress has posted a revealing Instagram. Berry doesn't shy away from going a little bare on occasion.
