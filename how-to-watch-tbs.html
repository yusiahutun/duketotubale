You don’t need a cable subscription to tune into the biggest events in sports, including the ongoing March Madness tournament. Want to watch TBS and all of its programs without any hassle? Keep reading to learn how to watch TBS for free without cable.

TBS is an American cable television network owned by Warner Bros Discovery. The channel was formed in December 1976 as the national feed of Ted Turner’s Atlanta, Georgia, independent television station, WTCG. TBS became one of the first channels retransmitted via satellite to regional cable providers. By the early 2000s, TBS focused on comedic programming, including sitcoms like The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, Full House, Home Improvement, and Seinfeld.

Today, TBS offers viewers a mix of game shows, reality shows, and re-runs of live-action sitcoms, like Family Matters, Friends, The Big Bang Theory, Young Sheldon, and Modern Family. The channel also airs sports events, including Major League Baseball, the National Basketball Association, the Stanley Cup playoffs, the NCAA men’s basketball tournament, and more.
