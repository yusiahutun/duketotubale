Sarah Jessica Parker looks up to designer L'Wren Scott

 Posted on September 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  310 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

    Dressed in one of L'Wren Scott's colourful creations, Sarah Jessica Parker clearly has a lot of respect for the renowned fashion designer - in fact you could even say she looks up to her.At least that's how it appeared at at a fashion event in New York on Thursday.Wearing a rainbow coloured dress, complete with matching coat and bag, Sarah was on hand to present the coveted Lord and Taylor Oracle award to L'Wren, who has been dating rock legend Mick Jagger since 2001. When the pair posed for photos, they made light of their height difference - Sarah stands at 5ft 3in to L'Wren’s statuesque 6ft 5in.

    Sarah stayed true to her Sex and the City character Carrie Bradshaw, by teaming her vibrant dress with a pair of black Manolo Blahnik heels. By her side L'Wren looked striking in a shimmering black dress and statement earrings. star Renee Zellweger also arrived at the event which was held at one of New York's hottest venue, the Cipriani Wall StreetForty-three-year-old Renee looked svelte and stylish in a grey, off the shoulder bodycon dress by Carolina Herrerra, which was also a tribute to the designer she was honouring. Renee also presented the Venezuelan fashionista with the Superstar Award during the event.

    Carolina, 73, sported a sophisticated navy blue and black outfit as well as a glittering pair of earrings and bold red lipstick.Also present at the prestigious event was Chanel maestro Karl Lagerfeld, actress Viola Davis and Nepalese designer Prabal Garung. Thirty-eight-year-old Prabal was the man behind the Duchess of Cambridge's pretty white and purple patterned dress, worn during her recent Jubilee tour to South East Asia.
