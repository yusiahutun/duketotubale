Who is Chuck Norris?

Chuck Norris born Carlos Ray Norris on March 10, 1940, is an American martial artist and actor. he is a black belt in multiple martial arts disciplines including Tang Soo Do Brazilian jiu-jitsu and judo. Norris served in the United States Air Force before venturing into Hollywood.

He gained fame in the 1970s, particularly after his role in “Way of the Dragon” opposite Bruce Lee. Norris became a household name in the ’80s and ’90s starring in action films and the long-running TV series “Walker Texas Ranger.”

Apart from acting he is also a noted writer and philanthropist supporting charities like the Make-A-Wish Foundation. Norris has been married twice and has five children.

Who Are Chuck Norris Parents?

Chuck Norris was born to Wilma (née Scarberry) and Ray Dee Norris in Ryan Oklahoma on March 10, 1940. His father Ray Dee Norris was a World War II Army soldier mechanic bus driver and truck driver. When Chuck was sixteen his parents divorced. He later moved to Prairie Village Kansas and then to Torrance California with his mother and brothers. Norris has stated that he has Irish and Cherokee roots and was the oldest of three brothers.

Chuck Norris Career

Chuck Norris born Carlos Ray Norris is an American martial artist and actor who has been active since 1968. He initially gained fame in the martial arts world winning numerous championships and even founding his own discipline Chun Kuk Do. His Hollywood journey began with a minor role in “The Wrecking Crew” in 1969 but he shot to fame after starring opposite Bruce Lee in “Way of the Dragon” in 1972.

Norris became a popular action film star in the late ’70s and ’80s with hits like “Good Guys Wear Black” “Missing in Action” and “The Delta Force.” In the ’90s he transitioned to television starring in the long-running CBS series “Walker Texas Ranger” which aired from 1993 until 2001. Apart from acting Norris is a noted writer and has endorsed several products most notably Total Gym exercise equipment.

What is Chuck Norris Ethnicity?

Chuck Norris has often stated that he has Irish and Cherokee roots. However, a deeper look into his ancestry reveals a more complex ethnic background. Norris’s ethnicity includes English with smaller amounts of Scottish Welsh and German. There are also remote traces of French Swedish and Dutch ancestry.

Although Norris has claimed both of his parents were of half Irish and half Native American ancestry no Native American heritage has been verified in publicly available family trees. Most of his recent ancestors including his paternal grandfather were U.S.-born and his family tree contains mostly English surnames and English-born distant ancestors.
