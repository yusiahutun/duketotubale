    No one was at a loss for a fun gathering out in the Hamptons over the long weekend. Rosario Dawson took over the Surf Lodge boutique for a 2-day pop-up with her 5-year old brand Studio 189. The beach-friendly kimonos and tie-dye pants help support women and artisans in Ghana. Bob Moses was the first of many performances on the deck, DJing for a packed house. Guests including Nina Agdal, Quincy Davis, Arlenis Sosa enjoyed Grey Goose's new summer cocktail, Le Grand Fizz, while the sun went down. Meanwhile, Revolve Clothing, known for their lavish trips around the world, landed in Bridgehampton for the month at a sprawling mansion where Hailey Baldwin hosted the kickoff 4th of July party. Top models like Romee Strijd and Jasmine Tookes rang in the holiday by the pool, while influential guests like Tash Oakley, Devin Brugman, Cara Santana and more showed off summer's must-have looks from the e-commerce site.
