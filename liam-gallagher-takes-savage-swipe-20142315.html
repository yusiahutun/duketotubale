Liam Gallagher takes savage swipe at brother Noel's height on Jonathan Ross show

 Posted on March 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  260 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Liam Gallagher, appeared on The Jonathan Ross show yesterday, as celebrated his 47th birthday.

The singer, who released album Why Me? Why Not. this week, got candid about his troubled relationship with older brother Noel Gallagher, 52, on the show.

Jonathan said he thinks the brothers have changed places and Liam has gone from being the "unreasonable" one to the "reasonable sibling.

Liam responded: "I don't know what it is with him at the moment, man.

"I don't know why we split up – I'd like to know."

Jonathan then said Liam "must know" why Oasis broke up.

Liam replied: "Maybe I was about to rock and roll for him and that.

"And I might have cancelled a few gigs and stayed up a bit late than I should have some nights."

"But we're not S Club f***ing 7," Liam added.

Jonathan then quizzed Liam about track One Of Us on his new album, and wondered if some of the lyrics were about Noel.

Liam said the track was about "brotherhood" and stressed how much he'd like Oasis get back together and for them both to make up.

Jonathan said: "If you were in the band now, you're 47 now, you're an older man, Noel's an older man – he's older than you isn't he? A little bit?"

Liam savagely responded: "Yeah, he's smaller as well."

Jonathan reasoned: "You're not helping matters."

Jonathan did manage to get a complement out of Liam though, with the rocker admitting Noel is a "great songwriter".

Please Don't Look Back In Anger, guys!
