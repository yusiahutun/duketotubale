The Name

Nadiad derives its name from the word ‘nadi’, which means river in the Gujarati language. The city is situated near the banks of the river named Kharicut.

Geographic Location

Nadiad is located in the Kheda district of Gujarat and lies approximately 35 kilometers southeast of the city of Ahmedabad.

Historical Significance

Nadiad has a rich historical background dating back to the 8th century. It has witnessed the rule of various dynasties, including the Gupta Empire, Chalukyas, Mughals, and British.

FAQs

1. What is the population of Nadiad?

Nadiad has a population of approximately 225,000 people.

2. What is the best time to visit Nadiad?

The best time to visit Nadiad is during the winter months of November to February when the weather is pleasant and conducive for exploration.

3. Are there any famous educational institutions in Nadiad?

Yes, Nadiad is well-known for its educational institutions, including the renowned Dharamsinh Desai University and the prestigious Gujarat Vidyapith.

4. What are some popular landmarks in Nadiad?

Some popular landmarks in Nadiad include the Santram Mandir, the Swaminarayan Temple, and the Shri Rugnath Temple.

5. Does Nadiad have any annual festivals?

Yes, Nadiad is known for its vibrant festivals, with the Janmashtami and Holi celebrations being particularly famous among locals and tourists.

6. What are some local delicacies to try in Nadiad?

When in Nadiad, make sure to try the famous Nadiadi Bhakri, Surti Locho, and Fafda Jalebi, which are all popular local delicacies.

7. Is Nadiad well-connected to other cities in Gujarat?

Yes, Nadiad has good connectivity via roadways and railways, making it easy to travel to and from other cities in Gujarat.
