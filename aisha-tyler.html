Early Life and Education of Aisha Tyler

Aisha Tyler was born on September 18, 1970, in San Francisco, California, the United States to  Robin Gregory, a teacher, and James Tyler, a photographer.

Tyler attended  McAteer High School in San Francisco. She graduated from Dartmouth college in 1992 and she was a member of  The Tabard, a co-ed fraternity.
