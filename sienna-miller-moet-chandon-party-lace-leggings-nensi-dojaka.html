The actress has been loving unique hosiery recently

    When Sienna Miller attends a party, you know it's going to be a sartorial sensation. 

    Her outfit for the Moet & Chandon Holiday Season Celebration at the Lincoln Center in New York last night was testament to that fact. Although it's been many years since she popularised the boho-chic aesthetic which had every fashion aficionado and TopShop girlie swooning in the early 2000s, the one thing that definitely hasn’t changed over time is her ability to inspire our fashion choices.

    RELATED: 10 easy ways to embrace boho style in your wardrobe

    Sienna Miller has been experimenting with daring hoisery lately 

    The star donned a striking LBD look, which was anything but predictable for the soirée. Sienna went for a full look from the Nensi Dojaka Spring/Summer 2023 runway, which consisted of a micro-mini black dress with an oversized keyhole cut out and coordinating leggings. The bottom half of her outfit proves that Sienna has been loving an unusual tights situation as of late (feast your eyes on the cutout socks she wore to Claridge's last month.) This time she wore Nensi’s lace leggings complete with stirrups to attend the champagne hosted bash. 

    Nensi Dojaka has become a bona fide Fashion Editor favourite this year. The London-based Albanian brand is the name on everyone's lips at the moment after it was nominated for a BFC Foundation Award at the 2022 Fashion Awards. The accolade, "recognises an emerging designer from those receiving support from the BFC Foundation in this year who over the last 12 months has had a major creative impact on global fashion." Singer Rita Ora opted for one of Nensi’s signature sheer chiffon dresses to attend the awards last night.
