The London native has 25 years of professional singing experience under her belt but chose to abandon this for a life as an ashram yoga teacher in the state of New York.

According to New York based yoga society Ananda Ashram, 'Reverend Kim Lesley has practiced her ministry in rehab centres in NYC incorporating spiritual counselling, prayer and healing meditation'. 

The society, founded in 1964, is an Indian centre for religious retreat, located just one hour away from New York City.

Jasmine Cephas Jones is the daughter of Ron Cephas Jones and Kim Lesley.

An award-winning actress in her own right, like her father she boasts an Emmy and has an affinity for theatre production, having starred in Hamilton - where her album contribution got her a Grammy.

Born in London, Jasmine was raised in the United States and attended the Berklee College of Music, the largest independent college of contemporary music in the world.

She's also ventured into the world of film and television - her most notable credits including roles in Blue Bloods and police procedural crime drama Unforgettable.
