Melissa Suzanne George (born 6 August 1976) is an Australian actress. A former national rollerskating champion and model, George began her acting career playing Angel Parrish on the Australian soap opera Home and Away (1993–1996). After moving to the United States, George made her feature film debut with a supporting role in Dark City (1998). This was followed by parts in The Limey (1999), Mulholland Drive (2001), Sugar & Spice (2001), and Down with Love (2003).
