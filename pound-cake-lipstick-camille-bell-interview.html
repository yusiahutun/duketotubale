I Asked My Fianc for These 8 Valentines Day Gifts, Starting at $24

 Posted on June 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  505 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

I’ve spent the majority of Valentine’s Days alone. While that sounds depressing, it wasn’t all that bad. I spent the day with girlfriends (galentines, anyone?), floral arrangements from my dad, and even a Taylor Swift dance party. But this year, I actually have someone to spend February 14 with, and spoiler alert: He’s my fiancé (as of a few days ago) and has already asked me what I want for V-Day.

It wasn’t difficult coming up with products I’ve been eyeing. How could it be with Jennifer Anniston’s glitzy Baublebar ring, a pretty pink Kate Spade satchel, or the insanely popular Stanley tumbler? But, being the good fiancée I am, I included a range of price points, starting at $24 — and you can shop them below.     

8 Must-Have Valentine's Day Gifts

Kate Spade Madison Saffiano Leather Medium Satchel

It’s hard to pass up this pretty pink tote. The soft color is repeatedly showing up in celebrities’ closets because it’s effortlessly beautiful. Details like a drop handle, detachable crossbody strap, spacious interior, and streamlined silhouette make it practical and functional. Better yet, its $135 price tag is a fraction of the original $429 cost.   

Baublebar Mini Alidia Cubic Zirconia Ring

As I mentioned, Aniston is a proud owner of this expensive-looking ring, but did you know that Julia Roberts also owns it? It doesn’t come as a surprise being that the coveted accessory sparkles and stacks with ease. It comes in sizes 5 to 11, includes cubic zirconia baguette stones, and gold hardware.

Stanley Quencher H2.0 Flow State 40-Ounce Tumbler 

You can’t go wrong with the internet’s favorite cup. The 40-ounce Stanley Quencher has racked up 606.1 million views on TikTok thanks to its double-wall vacuum insulation that keeps drinks cold for hours, vibrant hues, and rotating lid. I can’t get enough of the clean ivory hue, which is perfect for any aesthetic.  

Pound Cake Cake Batter Liquid Lipstick

This liquid lipstick is next level. Pound Cake (which is one heck of a name for a beauty brand), created a vibrant multitasking lippie that stays put while nourishing the lips. I’ve worn this product through multiple dinners, and it doesn’t budge. The six shades allowed me to find my ideal red, while a vanilla cake fragrance makes it enjoyable to wear. 

Hanky Panky Assorted 2-Pack Boyshorts

If there’s ever a time to receive lingerie, it’s Valentine’s Day — whether you buy it for yourself or gift it. I’m Hanky Panky’s number one fan. The brand’s undies are next level, delivering a barely there wear that never shows through clothing. The boyshorts are specifically comfy, providing the right amount of coverage via soft, flirty, and stretchy lace.
