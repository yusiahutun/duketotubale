Why Rosie Huntington-Whiteley is staying away from plastic surgery

 Posted on April 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  240 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley refuses to have cosmetic surgery "yet". The model and businesswoman says she finds the idea "terrifying" and wants to grow old "naturally".

“It’s a personal decision for everyone, but for me I find it terrifying,” she told The Sunday Times Style. “As I age, I would prefer to look natural. The idea of putting something permanent in my face isn’t for me.”

She dismissed speculation that her famously full lips have been enhanced with filler. “I’ve never done anything to them," she said. "I’ve got my dad to thank for my lips! In fact, at school I used to get called tit-lips because I had no boobs and big lips.”

Huntington-Whiteley, who has been open about how difficult it was to get back into shape following the birth of her son, now lives by intermittent fasting, an approach also adopted by Jennifer Aniston.

“I eat all my food in an eight- to 10-hour window. I’ll have a matcha latte with oat milk at 8am — that’s the most LA thing about me — and then I try to finish eating between 4pm and 6pm," she explains. "It gives my body a chance to digest everything. Of course, if I go out for dinner, I shift it to later. I’m flexible, you’ve got to live.”
