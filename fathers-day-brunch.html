    This year, Father's Day is Sunday, June 18—which means it's right around the corner. Check out our carefully curated Father's Day gift guide, peruse some Father's Day cards (you can even add a good dad joke inside), and then plan out your ultimate Father's Day brunch.

    But skip the crowds! You can make a wow-worthy spread in the comfort of your own home. (And Dad will love it.) We've compiled a big list with all of Dad's favorite sweet and savory breakfast and lunch dishes. Trust us, any one of these is sure to impress.

    1

    The Ultimate BLT Sandwich

    Becky Luigart-Stayner

    We took dad's favorite sandwich and upgraded it in every possible way: Homemade focaccia bread to replace the store-bought stuff. Oven-roasted tomatoes instead of a cold slice of beefsteak.

    Fresh arugula instead of iceberg. And crispy pancetta instead of your standard bacon. Topping it off? Home pickled onions. One bite and he'll flip for it.
