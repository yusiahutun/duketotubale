1. IMAX 20th Century - 20th Century Building

You can watch movies in Imax Nairobi at Anga Imax Cinema located in the 20th Century Building along Mama Ngina Street. The Imax 20th Century Cinema offers unmatched movie experience making it the most adored movie center. Some of the top features include top-notch facilities (dual projection, reflexive screens, perfectly-tuned sounds, and laser-linked audio), customized theater experience, and the latest 3D movies in Nairobi.

The Mama Ngina Street Imax theater is open from Monday to Sunday. The movies listed this week (July 3rd to 9th) include Solo: A Star Wars Story, Pacific Rim Uprising, Incredibles 2, Deadpool 2, Jurassic Park: Fallen Kingdom, Avenger: Infinity War, and Ant-man and The Wasp. Note that there are movies that all family members can watch at the Imax 20th Century. You can buy the Imax movie tickets online and then collect them at the Cinema. Remember, there is a no-refund and no-exchange policy on tickets purchased online. Therefore, ensure you research before booking a movie. Also, you get to select the movie, the show time, and type.
