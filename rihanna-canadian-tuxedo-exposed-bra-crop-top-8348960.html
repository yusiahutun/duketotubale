Rihanna Just Spiced Up the Canadian Tuxedo With an Exposed Bra and a Tie-Front Top

 Posted on February 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  280 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Rihanna's postpartum wardrobe is proving to be just as good as her maternity-wear. Just days after stepping out for her boyfriend A$AP Rocky's birthday in a tights-as-pants look with a bustier corset top and Balenciaga pantaboots, the new mom was at it again with another stellar date night outfit. 

On Friday, Rih and A$AP were photographed exiting their New York City hotel for a second parents' night out this week, and for the occasion, the singer put a spicy twist on the standard Canadian tuxedo. Wearing an oversized chambray shirt, which was left completely unbuttoned, as well as a matching pair of high-waisted baggy jeans, Rihanna broke up all of that denim with a white midriff-baring top that tied in the front and a lacy black bra underneath. To draw attention to her exposed neckline, Rihanna added layers of diamond-and-gemstone necklaces that plunged past her chest, while a pearl cocktail ring, square-shaped sunglasses, and black pointed-toe heels rounded out her look. 

She kept her glam simple, styling her brunette hair in wet waves and swiping on a nude lip. 

Back in August, it was revealed that Rihanna secretly gave birth to her and Rocky's second child, a son named Riot Rose Mayers. With the arrival of Riot and the couple's 1-year-old son RZA, Rihanna and A$AP's relationship has reportedly only gotten better. “Rihanna and A$AP are great right now. They’re so excited to grow their family and are so united when it comes to parenting. They have so much fun together and are very in love," a source previously told Entertainment Tonight. "They already shared a special connection, and parenting together has made their bond even stronger.”
