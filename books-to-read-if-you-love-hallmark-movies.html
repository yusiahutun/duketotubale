As Seen on TV

1

As Seen on TV

Now 31% Off

Credit: Amazon

Struggling freelance writer Adina Gellar is working as a barista and spin instructor to stay afloat, until she pitches the perfect story to an online magazine. This story could change her career, and her life. She heads to the small town of Pleasant Hollow, which has been targeted by a big-city real estate developer, in search of her story, only to find that the town is nothing like what she's seen in Hallmark movies and shows like Gilmore Girls and Virgin River (there's not even a bakery!). Can she turn things around in time to find a story?

Book Lovers

2

Book Lovers

Now 35% Off

Credit: Amazon

Nora, a tough-as-nails literary agent who normally finds herself on the wrong side of the romantic storyline, travels to Sunshine Falls, North Carolina, with her pregnant sister, Libby. Libby plans a small-town bucket list vacation (save a local business! date a local!), which takes a turn when Nora encounters her big-city rival, Charlie. What transpires is heartwarming, poignant, and laugh-out-loud funny.

3

Nora Goes Off Script

3

Nora Goes Off Script

This new release follows "Romance Channel" screenwriter Nora Hamilton, who turns a messy divorce into the best screenplay she's ever written. During the film shoot (which takes place at Nora's 100-year-old house) she befriends actor Leo Vance who, once the film wraps, offers to pay her $1,000 per day to stay at her house for another week. She could use the money and senses a sadness about him, so she agrees. A week isn't that long after all, though it is long enough to fall in love...

4

The Cheat Sheet

4

The Cheat Sheet

Credit: Amazon

Hallmark movie fans will love Sarah Adams's sweet rom-c0ms. The Cheat Sheet is a friends-to-lovers story about dance teacher Bree and her professional quarterback best friend Nathan who have to pretend to be dating in the weeks leading up to the Super Bowl. Sarah's next novel, When in Rome, debuts in September.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Credit: Amazon

Cass, a driven law student, wakes up after a car accident puts her in a coma talking about a mystery man named Devin Bloom. In Cass's mind, she and Devin had been dating for months before her accident, but none of her family or friends have heard of him, and there's no record of them in her calls or text messages. And yet, she draws vivid pictures of him and recalls specific memories of their time together, until one day she stumbles upon him in a local flower shop. The story takes the reader in some unexpected directions as Cass and Devin try to uncover the mystery of their connection and save the flower shop. (Available July 5)

6

Unleashing Mr. Darcy

6

Unleashing Mr. Darcy

A twist on Pride and Prejudice for dog lovers, Unleashing Mr. Darcy follows heroine Elizabeth Scott, who heads to London to assist a British dog show enthusiast after losing her job. Complications arise when Elizabeth butts heads with one of the judges, Donovan Darcy, a Cavalier breeder. Hallmark adapted the book into a movie of the same name in 2016.

The Kiss Quotient

7

The Kiss Quotient

Helen Hoang's The Kiss Quotient follows math whiz Stella Lane, who has built a successful career creating algorithms. Stella is ready for success in her romantic life too but, because she has Asperger's, decides she needs some practice. So, she hires professional escort Michael Phan to help and soon, sparks fly.

The Beach House (Book 1)

8

The Beach House (Book 1)

Best-selling author Mary Alice Monroe's The Beach House follows Cara, who returns to her family's home after being away for twenty years, reconnecting with family and rekindling a past romance. Hallmark's adaptation stars Andie MacDowell, Minka Kelly, and Chad Michael Murray.

9

The Worst Best Man

9

The Worst Best Man

Hailed as one of the best romance novels of 2020, Mia Sosa's The Worst Best Man follows jilted bride and wedding planner Carolina Santos, who must work with her loathsome ex-fiance's brother on a business deal. A mutual attraction begins, but will they be able to put the past behind them?

A High-End Finish (A Fixer-Upper Mystery, Book 1)

10

A High-End Finish (A Fixer-Upper Mystery, Book 1)

Perfect for bookworms who also appreciate a good home makeover, Kate Carlisle's Fixer-Upper Mysteries revolve around small town contractor Shannon Hammer. In the first installment, A High-End Finish, Shannon must solve a mystery in order to clear her name after she's suspected of murder. Hallmark adapted the books into a series of films starring singer Jewel.

The Bookshop of Second Chances

11

The Bookshop of Second Chances

Jackie Fraser's The Bookshop of Second Chances (a Country Living Front Porch Book Club pick!) follows down-on-her-luck Thea Mottram as she travels to a seaside village in Scotland after she inherits her late uncle's cottage, and soon finds herself in a battle of wills with the cantankerous local bookseller.

My Gal Sunday

The Irresistible Blueberry Bakeshop & Cafe

14

The Irresistible Blueberry Bakeshop & Cafe
