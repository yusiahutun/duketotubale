Biography

Stephnie Weir was born in Odessa, TX on November 28, 1967. Known best for her six-year-long association (in the early 2000s) with the Madtv sketch comedy series, she also appeared in such television shows as Big Day, Weeds, and Modern Family. She became known for her comedic impressions of Anna Nicole Smith, the Olsen Twins, and numerous other celebrities. After graduating from Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, she began performing with Second City, a renowned Chicago sketch comedy troupe.
