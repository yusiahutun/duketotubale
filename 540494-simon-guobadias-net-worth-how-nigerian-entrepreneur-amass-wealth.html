Who is Simon Guobadia?

Simon Guobadia was born in Nigeria on June 2, 1964, to Stanley Guobadia (father) and Martina Guobadia (mother). He completed his elementary and high school education in Nigeria.

Simon attended Loyola College in Oyo State, Nigeria, for five years and then joined Government College Ughelli in Delta State, Nigeria.

In 1992, he dropped out of college and immigrated to the United States. In 1993, Guobadia graduated with a BBA in accounting from the University of District Columbia in Washington, DC.

Simon is a proud father of six. He was previously married to Falynn Guobadia (nee Pina), a star on the reality TV show The Real Housewives of Atlanta. On November 25, 2022, Guobadia married Porsha Williams, and in February 2024, she filed for divorce.
