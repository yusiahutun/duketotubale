Where did they film Lonesome Dove?

The filming of the Lonesome Dove miniseries happened at the Moody Ranch in the south of Del Rio, Texas, United States. Since the show had many scenes, the producers also filmed it on other Texas and New Mexico ranches.

It took around 90 days for the shooting of the miniseries. Also, the directors and producers used real ranch horses to ensure authenticity. One of Lonesome Dove cast members, Tommy Lee Jones, refused to use a stunt double for all his riding scenes because he owns a ranch in Texas.
