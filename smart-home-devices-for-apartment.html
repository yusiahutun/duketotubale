7 Smart Home Devices for Your Renter-Friendly Apartment or Home

 Posted on May 18, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1123 words  |  Larita Shotwell

What is the best way to make any space feel like home? Renters often find themselves with little choice regarding what they can do when renting from landlords who aren't always willing or able to accommodate their needs and wants.

But, there are some ways that you can make your rental feel more like home without breaking the bank or your lease agreement.

One way is to invest in smart home devices that can help make your life easier and your space more comfortable with little to no effort.

1. Smart Locks

Being a renter often means having to deal with the headache of lost keys or waiting for your landlord to let you in when you're locked out. Smart locks can help take away that stress by allowing you to lock and unlock your door using your smartphone.

With a smart lock, you can also grant access to your home to family, friends, or service providers without having to give them a physical key.

You can also check to see who entered your home and when giving you an extra layer of security depending on the model you choose.

Don't worry about your landlord—most smart locks can be easily removed and reinstalled, making them a renter-friendly option.

2. Smart Thermostat

One of the biggest complaints that renters have is not being able to control the temperature in their space. With a smart thermostat, you can take matters into your own hands and enjoy a comfortable home without racking up a high energy bill.

Not only will you be able to adjust the temperature to your liking, but you can also save money on your energy bill each month.

Most smart thermostats come with an app that allows you to control the temperature from anywhere, so you can always come home to a comfortable space.

The best part is that many smart thermostats can be easily installed without professional help, making them an excellent option for renters. It's a small price to pay for a lot of comfort.

3. Smart Air Conditioner

If you live in a hot climate or your rental doesn't have central air, then a smart air conditioner is a must-have.

With a smart air conditioner, you can cool your space quickly and efficiently without having to worry about the noise level or energy consumption.

You can also set it to turn on and off at certain times or temperatures, so you can always come home to a cool and comfortable space.

A smart air conditioner is also a great option if you have allergies or asthma, as many come with filters that can help remove allergens and pollutants from the air.

Don't suffer through another hot and sticky summer; get a smart air conditioner and take control of your comfort.

4. Smart Lighting

Who doesn't love coming home to a well-lit space? With smart lighting, you can set the perfect ambiance for any occasion.

You can also save money on your energy bill each month as many smart light bulbs are more energy-efficient than traditional options.

Not only can you choose the perfect light setting for any situation, but you can also control your lights from anywhere using your smartphone.

With smart lighting, you can create the perfect ambiance for any occasion and save money on your energy bill each month.

You can also set your lights to turn on and off at certain times, or when you enter and leave your home, so you never have to worry about coming home to a dark space.

And if you have guests over, you can use smart lighting to create the perfect mood for entertaining.

There are many different types of smart lighting available, so you can find the perfect option to fit your space and needs.

5. Smart Plugs

What's a smart home without a few smart plugs? Smart plugs are one of the most versatile and affordable ways to make your home smarter.

With a smart plug, you can control any electrical device in your home using your smartphone.

You can also set timers and create schedules, so your devices are only turned on when you need them.

Smart plugs can save energy and money as you can easily turn off devices that are not in use.

They are an easy and affordable way to make your home smarter with a minimal investment.

What's more, many smart plugs come with features like surge protection and energy monitoring, so you can rest assured that your devices are safe and running efficiently.

6. Smart Speaker

How nice would it be to have a personal assistant in your home? With a smart speaker, you can enjoy the convenience of having a virtual assistant to help with tasks big and small.

You can ask your smart speaker to play music, set alarms, add items to your shopping list, and so much more.

And, if you have a smart home, you can also use your smart speaker to control your other devices. It's the perfect way to manage your entire home with just your voice.

Smart speakers are also a great way to keep your family entertained. You can use them to play music, podcasts, audiobooks, and more.

And, if you have kids, you can use a smart speaker to access educational content and help them with their homework. It's a win-win for everyone.

7. Smart Smoke Detector

If you live in a rental, you know that one of the most important things you can do is being prepared for emergencies. And while a traditional smoke detector is a must, a smart smoke detector takes things one step further by alerting you to potential dangers even when you're not home.

Since landlords are often reluctant to make major changes to apartments or let you hardwire new devices, a battery-operated smart smoke detector is the perfect solution.

With plenty of battery-operated smoke detectors on the market, you can find one that fits your needs and budget. And, since they don't require any wiring, they're easy to install and can be moved from one apartment to the next.

A Smarter Rental

When it comes to making your rental feel like home, there are plenty of smart devices that can help. There's a smart device for every need from lighting and climate control to security and entertainment. And, with so many affordable options on the market, there's no reason not to make your rental a smarter space.

There's no need to wait for your landlord to make changes to your rental. With a few simple additions, you can enjoy all the benefits of a smart home without sacrificing your security deposit. So, what are you waiting for? Start making your rental a smarter space today.
