18 of Her Best NYC (&amp; KC) Looks

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1463 words  |  Martina Birk

Taylor Swift is the musical mastermind of our generation (if you disagree please close your computer, go for a long drive and listen to Champagne Problems) but Taylor Swift’s street style also has quite an impact. Though music may be her main claim to fame, Taylor Swift is also a fashion girly. Who knows how many people she influenced back in 2014 to add a skater skirt and a pair of oxfords to their wardrobe? Taylor Swift’s street style is always fun, unique and even a little bit cryptic—you know she’s hiding Easter eggs in her handbag. 

If you’re a Taylor Swift fan (and if you’re not please go back to the first sentence of this article), you’ll know that Swift has been giving us fashion look after fashion look recently. She’s worn at least 28 different outfits on stage throughout her Eras Tour thus far and keeps adding more surprise looks as the tour continues. Her Eras Tour costumes demand attention—they include sequined bodysuits, huge princess gowns and fringe jackets, to name a few pieces. If you’re looking for statement outfit inspiration, the Eras Tour will act as your vision board. 

While her on-stage persona sparkles, Taylor Swift keeps her street style looks simple. This fall, Swift has been spotted numerous times around New York City (and even once in Kansas City) wearing a variety of effortlessly chic looks. From feminine skirt sets to cozy crewnecks, Swift brings her elevated taste to a very realistically wearable wardrobe. Essentially, Taylor Swift has provided us with all of the summer outfit inspiration we need. Keep scrolling for her best street-style looks. 

  • In New York City. October 15, 2023

    After staying out late the following night at the SNL after-party Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce went back out on the town for dinner. The rumored couple dined at The Waverly Inn, a West Village hot spot on Sunday night. Travis Kelce wore a corduroy set while Taylor Swift dressed up in a leather mini skirt and sheer long-sleeve top.

  • In New York City. October 15, 2023

    Taylor Swift and rumored boyfriend, Travis Kelce, both made surprise cameo appearances on Saturday Night Live. After the show, the duo arrived at the SNL after-party hand-in-hand. Swift wore a classy going-out look that featured a Versace corset top and houndstooth trench. She paired the look with black heels and a black shoulder bag. Travis Kelce kept his look cool and casual with brown trousers, white sneakers, and a canvas jacket.

  • In New York City. October 3rd, 2023

    Taylor Swift may no longer be dating a London boy but she still managed to serve major Princess Diana-style energy in New York City on October 3rd. The singer channeled the late Princesses’ sporty fashion sense with a causal look for an outing in Greenwich Village. Swift wore an oversized Shania Twain graphic T-shirt from Free People with a pair of biker shorts and paired it with a white “Still Here” baseball cap. She gave the outfit some girly flair by wearing green ruffled Free People crew socks with her New Balance sneakers. While the majority of Swift’s look retails for under $100, she did add one ultra-luxe piece to the ensemble with her Louis Vuitton box bag.

  • In Kansas City. September 24th, 2023

    Okay, if we’re being honest, this is more of a sideline style than a street-style look but it still counts! Swift broke the internet by cheering for rumored romantic fling Travis Kelce at the Chicago Bears vs. Kansas City Chiefs game on September 24th. She kept her look simple with a white tank, Chief’s jacket, and her signature red lip.

  • In New York City. September 21st, 2023

    Taylor Swift nailed dressing for transitional weather on a night out in New York City. She paired a chic off-the-shoulder top with neutral slacks and dressed the outfit up with red heels and lipstick. To (literally) top it off, she carried a chic black top-handle bag.

  • In New York City. September 19th, 2023

    An oversized sweater has never looked so cute. Taylor Swift played into an autumnal color palette while out in New York City. She paired a burnt orange sweater dress with knee-high leather boots and a forest green leather handbag.

  • In New York City. September 13th, 2023

    After winning the 2023 VMA for Video of the Year (and 8 other awards), Taylor Swift stepped out in a denim mini-dress to attend an after-party. In true Bejeweled fashion, Swift accessorized with crystal heels and a matching bag. She also wore a large gemstone necklace and sparkly cat-eye makeup.

  • In New York City. September 5th, 2023

    Taylor Swift took a more casual approach to her studio session look in early September. She stayed true to her minimalist personal style and opted for slacks and a black ribbed tank top. Swift accessorized with a camel-colored leather shoulder bag and a matching baseball hat.

  • In New York City. June 29th, 2023

    Swift picked a rare patterned piece from her wardrobe for a trip to Electric Lady Studios in the West Village. She wore a red floral corset-style tank top with sunglasses that looked like they came from her 1989 era. She kept the rest of her outfit simple by wearing dark denim shorts and black sandals.

  • In New York City. June 27th, 2023

    If the weather in your area stays relatively warm throughout the year, you can wear this outfit formula over and over again. Taylor Swift did a perfect job of balancing a girly blouse with a denim mini skort and loafers. The woven bag is the perfect warm weather accessory.

  • In New York City. June 26, 2023

    Swift started sporting a baseball cap back in June when she headed in for yet another session at Electric Lady Studios. The singer kept the masculine energy with an oversized button-down and loafers but added a feminine touch with ruffled socks and a shoulder bag.

  • In New York City. May 31st, 2023

    Even though Taylor Swift has an incredibly busy tour schedule, she still finds time to stop by Electric Lady Studios in the West Village. She wore a chic all-black tank dress for her most recent visit to the studio. She gave the look some shape by adding a black belt and matched it with black and gold Anine Bing sandals. She accessorized with a Mansur Gavriel box bag and oversized shades.

  • In New York City. May 24th, 2023

    Taylor Swift defined summer style in an adorable white skirt set. The singer spent yet another day at Electric Lady Studios in the West Village. What is she working on? Nobody knows! I do, however, know where her entire outfit is from. She chose a white eyelet skirt and tank set from Polo Ralph Lauren and paired them with a Mansur Gavriel box bag. And of course, it wouldn’t be a Taylor Swift look without red lipstick. 

  • In New York City. May 22, 2023

    Taylor Swift has nailed the effortlessly cool look and this neutral outfit proves it. She spent yet another day at Electric Lady Studios and left once the sun went down. She wore dark denim shorts and a white button-down from the brand R-13 and accessorized with a brown leather Ralph Lauren bag and strappy sandals. This outfit is easy to recreate (with more affordable options) and works as a fail-proof summer uniform. 

  • In New York City. May 16, 2023

    When Taylor Swift isn’t recording secret projects at Electric Lady Studios, she’s attending industry parties there. She left late at night with Matty Healy, who she was rumored to be romantically involved with. She wore a very casual NYU sweatshirt and tan sneakers.

  • In New York City. August 29, 2022

    Taylor Swift hinted at her “Midnights” album back in August of 2022 when she wore a midnight blue bodysuit, complete with sparkly stars, to the MTV VMA after party in New York City. She paired the fun bodysuit with a white faux-fur coat which she let hang off her shoulders. For an extra height boost, Swift wore silver sparkly platform heels. 

  • In New York City. June 11, 2022.

    Taylor Swift took a modern approach to suiting for an appearance at the Tribeca Film Festival in New York City in June of 2022. She attended to support All Too Well: The Short Film. Audience members were lucky enough to hear Swift perform the full 10-minute version of the song live on her acoustic guitar. 

  • In New York City. November 14, 2021.

    Taylor Swift opted for a business casual look for the SNL Afterparty in November of 2021 at L’Avenue in New York City. The star wore a blazer with black skinny jeans, a black long-sleeve shirt and black heeled boots for the party. She was perfectly coordinated with Blake Lively who attended the party in a black dress.
