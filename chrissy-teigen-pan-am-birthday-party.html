Chrissy Teigen Just Had the Cutest Retro Birthday Party on a Pan-Am Airplane

 Posted on June 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  409 words  |  Martina Birk

Chrissy Teigen forewent the ordinary birthday party when she celebrated her 32nd last night. Instead, she took her mom, her husband John Legend, and her closest friends on a '60s-themed ride aboard a Pan-Am airplane.

The flight wasn't real, though; the retro fête took place at the adult theme park, Pan Am Experience, in Pacoima, California. But that didn't make the event any less convincing.

Guests attended the soiree in full-out '60s garb. Teigen went for a hot pink peplum skirt suit with a beret, dotted scarf, and black gloves. Even Legend went all out with a plaid newsboy cap and beige vintage suit.

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

The couple's good friends, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West, were also present. Kim wore a baby pink skirt suit, white pumps, and oversized sunglasses and kept her hair brushed back in a bouffant, à la Jackie Kennedy. (The outfit was reminiscent of her look as "America's new first lady" on the cover of Interview magazine). Kanye didn't quite adhere to the dress code in his all-black attire, but he still looked sharp.

Kim Kardashian / Snapchat

Even Teigen's mom, Vilailuck, was adorably dressed in a striped cape. Her daughter Luna wasn't present for the grown-up party, but we would kill to see the one-year-old in her own tiny '60s costume.

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

The staff also adhered to the vintage theme, dressing in retro flight attendant and pilot uniforms.

Guests also enjoyed a game of in-flight bingo...

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

...which Kanye West won.

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

And there were vintage magazines (covered by stars like Jane Fonda and Jackie O) to flip through.

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

Fake cigarettes were available to play with, too.

Guests also had room to get up and dance, while Kris Jenner cheered along from her seat.

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

Naturally, John Legend took the mic and sang along.

Plus, the in-flight menu was actually impressive.

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

Last but not least, Chrissy was presented with a Mario and Nintendo Switch-inspired cake. (She recently tweeted about playing—and defeating—Super Mario Oddysey.)

Chrissy Teigen / Snapchat

Chrissy Teigen just took birthday goals to a whole new level.
