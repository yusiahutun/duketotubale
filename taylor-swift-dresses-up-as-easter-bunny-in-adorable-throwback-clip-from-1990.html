Taylor Swift dresses up as Easter Bunny in adorable throwback clip from 1990

 Posted on May 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  348 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Swifties got a special treat this Easter besides all the chocolate, as they were treated to an adorable throwback clip of baby Taylor Swift dressed as a bunny.

Since the start of her career, Taylor Swift has been dropping Easter Eggs for fans hiding hints, messages, and clues in her work. She’s now dropped the cutest Easter Egg of all, dating all the way back to 1990.

Taylor Swift looks adorable as a baby Easter bunny

To celebrate the holiday, Taylor’s team, known as Taylor Nation took to their Instagram page to share the cutest throwback of the music star in 1990.

In the adorable home video, dated March 14 1990, the Cruel Summer singer would have been just three months old as she was held by her mother, Andrea.

Baby Tay stared at the camera, waving her arms around in the cutest fluffy bunny outfit. “Dropping easter eggs since 1989,” the caption read. “What’s your favorite that you’ve found through the eras?”

Of course, fans flooded the comments of the video, and many were hopeful that once again, it was a clever hidden message.

“This seems like a normal post to celebrate easter…yet I feel like there are DEFINITELY easter eggs in this post…idk,” wrote one suspicious fan.

“Stoppppp I was gonna be productive today. Now I’m obsessing,” joked another.

A third gushed: “Baby Tay is just too precious!”

“The queen of Easter Eggs!” exlcaimed another. They got that one right!

Taylor had a ‘low-key Easter’ this year

Although we’re dying to know how Taylor and Travis Kelce spent their first Easter together, unfortauntely we’re not sure as they kept things low-key.

Recently, the two have popped up out and about in L.A, spending time together as they both enjoy a well-deserved extended work break.

It came after the two enjoyed a romantic getaway to The Bahamas providing us with all the adorable PDA.

After endless concerts around the world, the singer is taking a break from her Eras Tour until May, where she’ll be kicking off her European leg in Paris.
