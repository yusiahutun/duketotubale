Which Charging Station Is Best for Your PSVR2 Sense Controllers?

Most PSVR2 charging stations will charge your PSVR2 Sense controllers around the same time, between one and a half to two hours. But the best charging station offers additional features like powerful magnets for easy alignment of the controllers, LED charging indicators, and a PSVR2 headset stand.

The Collective Minds PSVR2 Showcase Charge and Display Stand is the best PSVR2 charging station for most people, as it's effortless to use, has a stand for the PSVR2 headset, and comes in cheaper than the official offering from Sony. However, nothing beats the official Sony PlayStation VR2 Sense Controller Charging Station when it comes to build quality and aesthetics.
