Nugent’s talents extended beyond television, gracing several memorable films, including Douglas Sirk’s productions “Magnificent Obsession” and “There’s Always Tomorrow.” Her performances as a wise-cracking tomboy and a daughter of iconic characters exhibited her ability to breathe life into diverse roles. Judy’s encounters with Hollywood legends like Rock Hudson, Cary Grant, and Gene Autry added rich layers to her memories of the era.

Nugent’s presence extended to the iconic “Mickey Mouse Club,” where she portrayed Annette Funicello’s companion Jet Maypen.
