The FACE of Italian Dining

 Posted on May 17, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  213 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Jimmy Fantin, Fantini’s Italian Restaurant

Jimmy Fantin thought about the restaurants he worked in and learned from in his high school and college days. He decided to see if he still had IT and began his search for a small location for his vision settling on a location on Federal Highway.

Last year, he put Stuart on the map with his authentic New Haven Style Apizza. Highly acclaimed by two famous pizza critics: Dave Portnoy of the blog Barstool Sports and Frank Gallo of Taste This with Frank Gallo on YouTube. He scored so high people dial in from all around the U.S. asking how to get to Stuart and make a reservation.

Jimmy worked in area restaurants in and around New Haven, Connecticut, and brought (besides Apizza) the best entrées he learned to cook to Martin and St. Lucie counties.

This year he is working diligently on his third expansion in the same Stuart location with plans to unveil an upscale expanded dining room and enlarged bar experience—beer, wine and craft cocktails along with more entrées, specials and desserts. Of course, his authentic Apizza will be there too.

Look for reservations in early December as Fantini will be the hottest ticket this season.
