12 most daring looks at the 2024 Golden Globes, from Taylor Swift and Timothe Chalamet, to Selena

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  329 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Golden Globes are a classic awards show, so it goes without saying that stars pull out their boldest looks for the evening.

At the 81st annual event on Sunday, celebrities walked the red carpet in stand-out suits, see-through dresses and audacious ensembles.

Here are a dozen of the most daring looks seen throughout the night.

1. Julia Garner

Garner’s sleeveless Gucci gown had all-over sequins, two straps that covered her chest and acted as a top, and a floor-length skirt.

She wore it with small gold earrings and a matching bangle.

2. Timothée Chalamet

3. Selena Gomez

The singer and actress’ metallic red gown had chest cut-outs decorated with black floral appliqués and an asymmetrical skirt that was knee-length on one side and nearly reached the floor on the other.

She wore the design, which was custom-made by Armani Privé, with diamond jewellery and an updo hairstyle.

Cuffing season: high jewellery artists make bold statements with diamond cuffs

4. Pedro Pascal

The Last of Us star arrived at the red carpet in Bottega Veneta and a black sling on his arm – which, he told reporters, he had injured during a fall.

5. Dua Lipa

It was also decorated with bone-shaped pieces made from gold and diamonds that referenced the shapes of the human skeleton.

6. Barry Keoghan

The Saltburn star’s custom Louis Vuitton outfit included a white button-up shirt underneath a red checkered jacket, matching trousers and pearl accessories.

7. Charles Melton

8. Taylor Swift

Swift arrived solo to the event in a shimmering green Gucci gown.

9. Hunter Schafer

Ear candy: stunning high jewellery earrings that stole the spotlight in 2023

10. Rosamund Pike

Pike’s strapless Dior dress had a corset bodice and full skirt, both of which were covered in the same appliqués that decorated its netted sleeves and neckpiece.

The actress completed the outfit with a black headpiece that featured a netted veil.

11. Margot Robbie

12. Heidi Klum

The supermodel turned heads in a red strapless gown with a high-slit skirt.
