Kim Kardashian Wears a Sheer Metallic Naked Dress on Instagram

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  305 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kim Kardashian rocked yet another naked dress on Instagram. For her latest NSFW look, the reality star wrapped up in a sheer metallic wrap gown, which she wore for a fitting, she explained in her caption. The KKW Beauty mogul didn't share where she's wearing the dress, but if ever it hits the red carpet, it's bound to turn heads.

The barely-there look consisted of extremely sheer, flowy fabric covered in brilliant crystals. And as if the dress couldn't get more provocative, the low-cut wrap design also includes a thigh-high leg slit. Kim is clearly pulling all the stops here.

The dress is by Ukrainian designer Yasya Minochkina, who proudly shared that Kim wore her dress on Instagram. It's part of the brand's spring 2018 collection and previously hit the runway in Moscow in October 2017.

Epsilon//Getty Images

Minochkina previously said on Instagram that her shimmery sheer dress is "perfect for Summer special occasions," but Kim, who wore it before the beginning of winter, loves to break fashion rules.

Kardashian-Jenner sisters have been sporting some nearly-naked dresses as of late. Last week, Kylie Jenner wore a figure-hugging beaded dress that was almost just as sheer as Kim's. The look, by Yousef Aljasmi, drew comparisons to Britney Spears' embellished bodysuit from her "Toxic" music video.

Days prior, Kendall Jenner also hopped on the trend at the British Fashion Awards in a gold beaded naked dress by Julien Macdonald. The 23-year-old model freed the nipple and wore nothing but nude underwear underneath the dress, and completed the look with transparent heels and gold rectangular earrings.
