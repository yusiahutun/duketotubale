Jodelle Ferland biography

The actress was born on 9th October 1994 on the East Coast of Vancouver Island, known as Nanaimo, British Columbia, Canada. Jodelle Ferland's parents are Marc Ferland (father) and Valerie Ferland (mother).

She has a Canadian nationality and a mixed-race ethnic background. Her ethnicity is a blend of English, Austrian, French and a little mix of Asia. She has two elder siblings who are successful individuals in the entertainment industry. Jodelle has an elder sister, Marisha Ferland (actress) and an elder brother Jeremy Ferland (musician).

Career

The actress began her career at the young age of two in various commercials and TV advertisements. Upon turning four years old, she made her acting debut in the film, Mermaids. She earned a nomination at the Daytime Emmy Award from the film, making her the youngest nominee.
