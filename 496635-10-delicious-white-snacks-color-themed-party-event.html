10 delicious white snacks for your color-themed party or event

 Posted on April 2, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1353 words  |  Martina Birk

A colour theme is a great idea to make your party more exciting. One of the top colour-themed party ideas is an all-white party, which can make your party appear classier and more elegant. But, of course, the success of your white party or event will depend on how well you execute it. At the top of things to take care of is food or snacks. But which are some of the white snacks for your color-themed party or event?

Serving white snacks at your all-white party or event will help keep your guests well entertained and satisfied. Additionally, they will help to complement your décor, making your party all the more exciting.

Top 10 white snacks for a colour party or event

There are plenty of snacks to serve at an all-white party or event, ranging from sugary snacks to healthy snacks, food items, and even white drinks. Below is a look at some of the delicious ones:

1. Popcorn

Popcorn is one of the most popular snacks you can serve at parties and events – from a birthday to a movie night, wedding, and business events. The best part is popcorn is white, making it the ideal snack for your all-white party or event. Additionally, you can go with various popcorn flavours, from salty to sweet, buttery, and others, to ensure your guests have the most fun.

Besides flavours, you can use popcorn to compliment your party or event décor. Top ideas include an all-white popcorn machine or stand. You can also serve your popcorn in white boxes or bowls.

2. White baked goods

Everyone loves baked goods, so you will have many fans if you serve them at your party or event. Additionally, some events and parties – such as birthdays and anniversaries -are incomplete without your favourite baked food – a cake.

When hosting an all-white party or event, a white cake is one of the must-have snacks. You can go with a simple white-flavoured cake with vanilla flavour or be bold and experiment with other flavours such as lemon, orange and lemon zest, and almonds. For the cake decoration, white cream will be perfect. However, you can pair the cream with another décor, such as white flowers (both real and sugar flowers), toppers, and sprinkles.

Besides cakes, other baked goods you can consider include cupcakes, bread, cookies, and doughnuts. For snacks that aren’t white, you can dip them in white cream frosting or pour powdered sugar on top of them to fit your party theme.

3. White candy

White candy is another top snack idea for your all-white party or event. Candy is especially a hit if you host a kids' birthday party or get-together, but it will also do when hosting an adult party. One of the top candy ideas for your party is to have a candy buffet table where your guests can treat themselves to sweet treats or even steal some to take home with them.

Some white candy snacks you can offer include gummy bears, rock candy gumballs, chocolate truffles, candy cane, and fudge. Besides candy, you can also serve other sugary treats on your buffet table, such as marshmallows, candyfloss, candied coconut, and other white packaged snacks you can find at the supermarket.

4. Sandwiches

If you are looking for a great idea all-white food menu for your party, sandwiches are a great idea. They are perfect for many events, including kids' parties, birthday parties, brunch gatherings, picnics, and business parties.

To fit your white colour theme, you should make your sandwiches using white bread. You can then use white or neutral sandwich fillings such as eggs, poultry meat, ham, and cheese. However, you can also paint your sandwiches a little with filings and toppings such as guacamole, cucumber, and leafy vegetables.

5. White chocolate

White chocolate is another one of the top white snack ideas for a party or event. You can serve the chocolate alone or use it to make other delicious treats. Top examples include chocolate desserts such as white chocolate mousse and chocolate fudge. You can even make a white chocolate drink which will be a delicious treat. Lastly, you can create a chocolate dip and make chocolate-covered strawberries, which are guaranteed to be a huge hit.

6. Pretzel and potato chips

Potato chips and pretzels are another good idea if you want more white snacks for a party or event. These are two of the most popular food items for snacking on the go and will be a massive hit at any party or event you are throwing.

When matching your colour theme, you can find many white pretzels brands at your local supermarket. However, for potato chips, you will need to find brands whose chips are more neutral in colour. Lastly, you can make some white dips to serve with your chips.

7. White vegetables

If you are health conscious, you can also serve vegetables as a snack for your party or event. And you can find lots of white or neutral-coloured vegetables, including cauliflower, white cabbage salads, white onions (for toppings), and mushrooms. Besides vegetables, you can also find some creative white food ideas for your party or event. For example, you can serve white rice, pasta, and sushi rolls.

8. Vanilla ice cream

White ice cream is a great treat to serve during your white-themed summer parties and events. It will also be a hit if you host a birthday party, kids' party, or event. The best thing about ice cream is that there are lots of cool and fun ideas you can serve it.

One of the top ones is to make ice cream sundaes. You can also use it to create desserts, for example, by serving ice cream alongside cake or using it as a filling for your baked goods.

9. White fruits

Fruits are another healthy snack you can serve at your party or event. You can find many white fruits to match your all-white theme, from bananas to pears, white watermelons, coconut, white blackberries, and currants.

You can also turn any coloured fruit white by covering it in a white dip. For example, you can cover strawberries and apples in white chocolate, sour cream, or yoghurt, creating delicious treats for your guests.

10. White drinks

Your all-white party cannot be complete without serving white snacks and drinks. And for the drink part, you can offer many creative white or neutral-coloured beverages. For example, you can offer milk, yoghurt, and milkshakes if hosting a kids' party or event. However, for an adult party, you can serve white wine or various white cocktails such as snowflake martinis, margaritas, daiquiri, and white Russian cocktails.

Planning a white-colour-themed party or even can be a fun experience. Of course, top among your host duties will be to select foods, snacks, and drinks that will keep your guests full and happy. The above are some of the top ideas for white snacks that you can use to make your party a hit. Despite being delicious, these snacks will also help to complement your party's décor, especially if you are going with a strict all-white theme.
