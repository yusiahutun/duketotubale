X Factor's Katie Waissel strips naked to show off toned body in fitness photoshoot

 Posted on March 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  352 words  |  Martina Birk

Katie Waissel has shared an inspiring Instagram post as she posed completely naked in a fitness selfie.

The 25-year-old singer, who shot to fame on The X Factor in 2010, flaunted her figure after her incredible transformation as she told fans about her fitness journey.

She posed in a sprint stance in the tasteful snap of her side profile.

Mum-of-one Katie shared the photograph with her 25,600 followers on Instagram.

She wrote: "With Every Stride I Feel Reborn, With Every Breath I feel Reconnected - In order to move forward, I had to step back, resetting my physical body, accepting that emotionally, I had a long road in front of me."

Katie was ill with suspected coronavirus in 2020, and she is currently attempting to overcome a persistent lung condition.

She continued: "Rehabilitating my current lung condition with the help and direction of specialists has been incredibly fascinating (and tough).

"Noting what has worked aerobically along with nutrition, proves my long-standing mantra - Be The Best Version Of Yourself From Within, The Outside Will Follow Suit."

She added: "So please look at this picture for exactly what it is, the road to recovery, completely bare, stripped back, honest, authentic, vulnerable, yet strong, focused and someone who had just completed a 5K with a Personal Best Time of 27mins (pretty proud for just three weeks into my rehab program!)"

Her devoted followers rushed to leave a comment, showering Katie with messages of love and support.

One said: "Wow you look incredible!"

While another penned: "Looking strong, healthy and beautiful", with a flame emoji.

"Wow absolutely stunning and beautiful", gushed a third with two heart emojis.

After the birth of her son Hudson, Katie decided to work hard on toning her body and overhauling her life during her fitness journey.

The star, who is now somewhat of an Instagram influencer, uses her account to keep her followers updated on her progress.
