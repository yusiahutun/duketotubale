Underboob-baring clothing has taken the fashion world by storm this year.

Demi Rose Mawby and Kim Kardashian are among the celebrities to try the trend.

But you don’t have to be a famous face to pull it off.

Oh Polly is selling a “underbust bikini” that leaves little to the imagination.

So would you be brave enough to try it?

Oh Polly’s “Spill The Tea” bikini would turn heads if you wore it on the beach.

The T-shirt style top features a slit beneath the cleavage, exposing plenty of underboob.

If that wasn’t racy enough, the bottoms are a thong fit.

Even though the two-piece is very risqué, it hasn’t stopped influencers from wearing it.
