Photos Of Michael C. Hall &amp; More Hollywood Life

 Posted on May 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  195 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

He’s back! Dexter Morgan will return in the limited series event ‘Dexter: New Blood,’ which premieres November 7 on Showtime. Michael C. Hall is reprising the fan-favorite role that he originated in the original Showtime series that premiered in 2006. Dexter was last seen living a life of anonymity as a lumberjack. ‘New Blood’ takes place 10 years after the events of the ‘Dexter’ season 8 finale.

Dexter is now living in Iron Lake, New York, hiding his identity under the name of Jim Lindsay, a local shopkeeper. He’s been able to keep his serial killing urges under control for a while, but he can’t resist the temptations forever. Dexter will cross paths with familiar and new faces in the new series.

Michael continues to play the role of Dexter Morgan to perfection. After leaving the character behind in 2013, Michael went on to star on Broadway in ‘Hedwig and The Angry Inch.’ He played JFK in an episode of ‘The Crown.’ Michael is also a member of the band Princess Goes to the Butterfly Museum. He married Morgan MacGregor in 2016. See photos of the rest of the ‘Dexter’ cast then and now.
