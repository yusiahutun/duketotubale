It was revealed earlier this week that Ashley Tisdale had split from her long-term boyfriend Scott Speer after two years together.

But the High School Musical starlet is certainly wasting no time getting herself back on the singles market.

Just a day after Ashley was spotted out and about with a mystery man, the 26-year-old actress has been seen spending the evening with Boys Like Girls frontman Martin Johnson.

The partying continues: Newly-single Ashley Tisdale headed out with Boys Like Girls singer Martin Johnson in Los Angeles on Friday night

Ashley donned a sexy black velour minidress as she and Johnson attended a Robin Thicke concert together in Los Angeles.

The duo were joined by friends including Hilary Duff's sister Haylie, and married actors Channing Tatum and Jenna Dewan for the evening out.

At the event, Ashley tweeted fans: 'With @HaylieK @jennaldewan and@channingtatum watching Robin Thicke. The night has just started :)'

What's so funny? The pair appeared to be in good spirits as they arrived at a Robin Thicke concert

Sexy: Ashley looked stunning in a black velvet minidress with sheer lace back panel

The evening out on Friday night came just days after it was revealed Ashley was once again single after splitting from video producer Speer.

'They had been on and off for a while. They tried to work it out but realised their relationship ran its course,' a source told Us Weekly.

Ashley and Scott began dating in spring 2009, a month after her split with musician Jared Murillo.

Split: It was revealed earlier this week that Ashley and music video producer Scott Speer had broken up

Scott had also directed the star's music videos for It's Alright It's OK and Crank It Up from her album Guilty Pleasure.

He has also worked with Paris Hilton, Jordin Sparks, David Archuleta and Blake Shelton.

There has been no direct mention of the split on Ashley's Twitter page, although she did mysteriously tweet last week: 'What goes around comes back around.'
