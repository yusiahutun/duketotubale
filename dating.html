Ruth Berhe (born July 2, 1995), better known by her stage name Ruth B., is a Canadian singer and songwriter from Edmonton, Alberta. She started by singing songs on Vine in early 2013. In November 2015, she released her debut extended play, The Intro. On May 5, 2017, she released her debut album, Safe Haven. It has gathered over 1 billion overall streams on Spotify as of October 2021. Her hit single "Lost Boy" has accumulated over 630 million streams on Spotify, and her YouTube channel has received a total of 340 million views as of October 2021.
