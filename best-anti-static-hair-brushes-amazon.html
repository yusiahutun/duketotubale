You’ve picked out the perfect outfit and you’ve got your makeup look planned out. You have a vision of what the entire thing is going to look like. All you need to do is style your hair. The brushes are usually made out of a combination of boar bristles or nylon to help stimulate your scalp and evenly distribute the oil on your scalp, so your hair grows healthier.
