Who Is Mackenzie Davis?

She is a Canadian actress living in New York City. She was born on April 1, 1987, in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, the daughter of a South African mother named Lotte and an English father named John Davis. Her mother is a graphic designer while her father is a hairdresser. Together they own a hair care company known as AG Hair Care. Founded in 1989, the family’s hair care line is the largest independent manufacturer of professional haircare products in Canada. The company is based in British Columbia, Canada.
