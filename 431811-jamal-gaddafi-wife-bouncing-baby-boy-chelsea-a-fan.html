Jamal Gaddafi, Wife Ahlam Faysal Welcome Bouncing Baby Boy: Chelsea Has a New Fan

 Posted on February 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  497 words  |  Aldo Pusey
  • Jamal Gaddafi shared a short clip and a snap as he held onto his newborn child named Emaad
  • The Journalist disclosed that the baby arrived well and in good health, referring to him as a miracle
  • The TV presenter's friends and fans shared lovely messages as they congratulated him and his wife on the great news

Jamal Gaddafi and his wife Ahlam Faysal have welcomed their thirdborn child, a bouncing baby boy.

The KTN TV presenter recently announced his wife's pregnancy while celebrating her birthday.

On Tuesday, November 2, Jamal went on his Instagram to announce that the baby had arrived with a cute post.

He shared a short clip, with the baby's face hidden, that showed the little one, who they have named Emmad, moving his arms and legs cutely while being prepared.

He also shared a photo of him holding on to the infant's hand as he began bonding with his newborn as early as possible.

The journalist showed his delight, noting the baby boy arrived well and in good health.

"I am delighted to announce that our precious little king Emaad and miracle arrived well and in good health. Alhamdulilah," he wrote.

An obedient child

Jamal prayed that Emaad may turn out to be one who obeys the teachings of Islam, respectful to the elders and is wise.

He also quipped that his favourite football club, Chelsea, had also found a new fan.

May Allah enable him to be a child who obeys the teachings of Islam and be respectful to his elders. May he be an intelligent child and full of wisdom. Him and the other kids. Chelsea has a new fan," he added.

The news delighted many of his fans, who congratulated him on his arrival.

Pregnancy announcement

As mentioned earlier, via his Instagram stories in October, Jamal shared a beautiful photo of his expectant wife as he celebrated her birthday.

He wrote on the photo that they were expecting a new family member and asked God to protect his wife through her delivery.

"Happy birthday to the mother of my kids. May God give you health and happiness. We are eagerly expecting a newcomer to our family. May God make it easy," read the note.

Judging from the photo which showed Faysal's baby bump, the mother of two was in her advanced stage of pregnancy.
