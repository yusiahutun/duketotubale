Biography

Arthur Ashe was born in Richmond, VA on July 10, 1943. Tennis star who was the first African-American member of the Davis Cup tennis team. He won three Grand Slams during his career: the 1968 US Open, the 1970 Australian Open, and the 1975 Wimbledon tournament. He contracted HIV from a blood transfusion and passed away from AIDS in 1993. He beat Jimmy Connors in the 1975 Wimbledon final match.
