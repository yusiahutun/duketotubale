Ashley Olsen spotted 'holding hands and snuggling' New York artist George Condo

 Posted on May 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  445 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ashley Olsen was spotted 'holding hands and snugging' with famed painter George Condo in New York over the weekend.

The 30-year-old fashion designer has sparked rumours she's dating the popular artist, 59, after they were seen getting cosy over dinner at the brasserie Balthazar in the Big Apple.

An eagle-eyed onlooker told the New York Post newspaper's Page Six column: 'It was obvious something was going on [between them]. It looked like she [Ashley] was dining with her grandpa, but they were holding hands and snuggling.'

New love? Ashley Olsen, 30, pictured  back in March, has been romantically linked to New York-based artist George Condo, pictured last year  

However, this isn't the first time the pair have set tongues wagging.  

They also sparked speculation of a romance two months ago when they were seen at the Mercer Hotel together.

Despite onlookers stating their arrangement looked 'romantic', a representative for George was adamant at the time that they had simply met up for a 'business meeting' and were not dating. 

Ashley is no stranger to dating older men, briefly romancing Bennett Miller, 49, until they called time on their relationship earlier this year.

Just like my twin: Ashley's twin sister Mary-Kate wed 49-year-old Olivier Sarkozy last November 

The actress-turned fashion designer also dated David Schulte, Justin Bartha, Greg Chait and Scott Sartiano, and was rumoured to have romanced Johnny Depp and Jared Leto, although neither relationship was ever confirmed.

Meanwhile, George's wife Anna, with whom he has two grown-up daughters Eleonore and Raphaelle, filed for divorce last year.

It seems Ashley could be following in the footsteps of her twin sister Mary-Kate when it comes to dating an older man.

Talented trio: Mary-Kate, younger actress sister Elizabeth and Ashley together at the opening of the twins' Elizabeth James flagship store in Los Angeles last month

Mary-Kate wed Olivier Sarkozy, 47, half brother of former French President Nicolas, last November. 

The pair first started dating in 2012 and, despite their 17-year age gap, they got engaged in 2014 and eventually tied the knot in November last year.

George Condo is a contemporary visual artist and recently collaborated with Kanye West, creating a series of paintings for the rapper's album My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy. 

Kanye, now 39, even commissioned Condo to hand paint a Hermes Birkin bag for his wife Kim Kardashian in 2013.
