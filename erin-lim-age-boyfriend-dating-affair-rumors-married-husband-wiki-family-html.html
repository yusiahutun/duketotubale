Erin Lim's Short Bio: Age To Profession!

Erin Lim, who is 27 years of age, was born on 14 April 1990 in California, the USA and raised in California. Erin sprouted a passion for media in her early days and later guided by passion, she graduated from Chapman University Dodge College of Film and Media Arts in 2013. 

She started her career in 2012 as a production assistant at the 38th Annual People’s Choice Awards. Erin is best recognized for her work for E! News as a correspondent in the E! News’ Snapchat show, The Rundown. The level of her recognition does not stop there, as she is not just famed for being known as an E! News correspondent in the U.S., but has her reach over several other Asian countries like the Philippines, Hong Kong, and plenty others.

She has also interviewed the likes of Justin Bieber on his Calvin Klein collaboration.

In 2018, Erin has gathered fame by attending many prestigious award ceremonies like 2018 SAG AWARDS, The 2018 Grammy Awards.

Hands-down a lady, driven by versatility and success!
