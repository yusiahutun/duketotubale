Sala Beckett is a renowned theater in Barcelona.

Sala Beckett, located in the Poblenou neighborhood of Barcelona, is a celebrated theater that has been captivating audiences for decades. Its rich history and commitment to promoting contemporary Catalan plays make it a cultural landmark in the city.

The theater was founded in 1984.

Sala Beckett was established in 1984 by Mercè Rodoreda, a prominent Catalan writer, with the aim of providing a platform for emerging playwrights to showcase their work. Since then, it has become a hub for experimental theater and avant-garde performances.

Sala Beckett is known for its innovative playwriting workshops.

One of the distinctive features of Sala Beckett is its renowned playwriting workshops. These workshops attract aspiring playwrights from around the world, offering them a unique opportunity to develop their skills under the guidance of experienced mentors.
