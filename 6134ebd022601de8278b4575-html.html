How to download and convert MP3 YouTube videos for free

 Posted on April 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  308 words  |  Larita Shotwell

YouTube is the biggest video sharing platform on the planet, but users don't only go to the site for video content.

There is an endless selection of music, sound effects and tracks, with entire channels having been created just for this purpose.

Many of you will therefore be keen to take these audio samples and tracks and listen to them offline or use them elsewhere, which is why we've put together an easy step-by-step guide to download audio from YouTube for free.

Downloading audio to an MP3 format is the easiest and most practical choice. Here are some ways that you can download audio for free from YouTube.

Download video audio using Converto

Convert allows you to easily download audio in an MP3 format from the site, and here's how.

Step 1: Copy and paste the YouTube URL to the Converto website and select the download button. The site will then search for your clip.

Step 2: Ensure you have selected MP3 from the dropdown and click 'Convert'.

Step 3: You will receive a notification when your file is ready to download. Click on the notification to download to your desktop.

Download a file using 320YouTube

This downloader can be used in two different ways.

Option 1 is to download the file directly through YouTube itself.

Step 1: In the URL of the video, you wish to download, type '320' before 'youtube.com'.

Step 2: Your browser will take you to the 320YouTube webpage, where you should then click 'Download MP3'.

The second option involves using the YouTube 360 site directly.

Step 1: GO to 320YouTube and past the URL of the video directly into the address box on the site and click 'Convert'.

Step 2: You will then see the video on the left side of the screen. Click 'Download MP3' to convert the video to an audio file.
