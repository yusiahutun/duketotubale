So, what do you need to know about Wayne Newton after his Bachelorette appearance? Check it out.  

Who is Wayne Newton?

Wayne Newton, real name Carson Wayne Newton, is a singer and entertainer. He was born April 3, 1942 in Norfolk, Virginia.

His love of music began in childhood. Wayne is said to have learned to play several instruments, including piano and guitar, at six years old. Eventually he began singing, making appearances at local clubs, theaters and fairs after his family relocated to Ohio and, eventually, Arizona.

Wayne and his brother Jerry formed a group called Rascals in Rhythm, appearing on ABC’s Ozark Jubilee and performing for then-President Dwight D. Eisenhower.

A Las Vegas, Nevada-based booking agent saw Wayne performing on a local TV show and reached out to invite them to audition. They were selected and began performing six shows a day for five years in Sin City.

Wayne signed with Capitol Records in 1963 to release his first album, Danke Schoen. Nearly 30 more albums have followed since, though Danke Schoen is perhaps his most famous.

In addition to his music career, Wayne has enjoyed a mildly successful acting career making cameos and taking roles in TV shows and films. He appeared in Licence to Kill, Ocean’s Eleven, Who’s Your Daddy?, The Hangover, Hoodwinked Too! Hood vs Evil and, most recently, Sharknado: The 4th Awakens.

Wayne Newton wife

Wayne Newton married Elaine Okamura in 1968, welcoming a baby girl called Erin Newton in July 1976. More than a decade after their daughter’s birth, Wayne and Okamura divorced.

He married Kathleen McCrone in 1994. Kathleen was working as a lawyer at the time.

Has Wayne Newton had plastic surgery?

Wayne Newton has never implicitly stated that he’s undergone plastic surgery. It’s clear that his face has changed in some way from his earlier years, but not which — if any — procedures he’s undergone.

Wayne Newton net worth

According to BankRate Wayne Newton’s net worth is about $100 million (£75.5m). Wayne has had his issues with the IRS, however.

In 1992 he declared bankruptcy and was believed to be about $20 million (£15.1m) in debt. He was sued by the IRS in 2005 with the agency alleging that Wayne and his wife owed them upwards of$1.8 million (£1.3m).

How did Wayne Newton make his money?

Wayne Newton made the majority of his money as a recording artist and Las Vegas performer. He also breeds Arabian horses. The Washington Post once estimated that he may make up to $10 million (£7.5m) per year as an entertainer in Las Vegas, Nevada.
