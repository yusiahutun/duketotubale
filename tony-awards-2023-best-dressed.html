The Tony Awards 2023: The best dressed

 Posted on February 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  273 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Getty Images

Lupita Nyong'o

WWD//Getty Images

The Tony Awards tends to be a red carpet that stars play pretty safe, but Lupita Nyong'o chose to push the style boundaries with her look, and it certainly paid off. The actress wore a silver breastplate underneath a chic black suit by Misha Japanwala, and added jewellery by De Beers.

Lea Michele

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

Embracing a floral and feminine look was Lea Michele in a strappy ballgown by Emilia Wickstead.

Jessica Chastain

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

Jessica Chastain injected some sunshine yellow onto the Tony Awards red carpet in a structured dress by Gucci which was complete with a dramatic cape.

Jodie Comer

Dominik Bindl//Getty Images

Jodie Comer took home a big award on the night, and was dressed for the occasion in a super simple, chic black gown by Thom Browne, which she wore with Ana Khouri jewellery.

Michelle Williams

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Adding a little sparkle to the occasion was Michelle Williams. The actress chose a metallic column gown by Louis Vuitton, keeping her accessorising to a minimum.

Ariana Debose

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Presenter Ariana Debose also embraced silver for the evening. Her dress was custom Prada, and she wore jewellery by De Beers.

Lorna Courtney

Dominik Bindl//Getty Images

Lorna Courtney was another attendee to embrace some florals for the evening, wearing a beautifully detailed gown by Markarian.

Kara Young

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Keeping things altogether more simple was Kara Young who chose a strapless, black gown with embroidered detail. She added some drama with a chunky choker necklace and purple lipstick.
