13 best thinking of you gifts: What to give a friend to show you care on any occasion

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  991 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Do you remember how you felt when you last received an unexpected bunch of flowers, or a surprise heartfelt card? It’s no secret that a small gesture can go a long way when someone is having a hard time, and a thinking of you gift is the perfect way to show you care.

If you know someone who needs a little pick-me-up, why not let them know you are thinking of them? Give them a thoughtful present that's sure to comfort them and maybe even put a smile on their face.

How we chose the best thinking of you gifts

  • Experience: After losing my mum in 2021, I have discovered how kind people can be in a time of need. A lot of the thoughtful gifts I received during that time are in this list. 
  • Price: A thoughtful gift needn't cost the earth, so I didn't want to feature super expensive gifts in this. 
  • Speedy delivery: You don't want to be waiting a long time for your gift to arrive to your friend in need, all of the inclusions in this edit have a speedy delivery option.
