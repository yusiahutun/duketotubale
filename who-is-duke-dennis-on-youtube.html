DUKE Dennis is an American YouTuber, gamer, and popular internet personality.

His basketball content has racked up more than 160 million views and he is considered one of the best YouTubers in that field.

Who is Duke Dennis?

Duke Dennis is an American YouTube personality and content creator, from Greenville, South Carolina.

He began his career as a streamer in 2013 and is one of the most popular gamers and YouTubers worldwide.

When he was younger he was an extremely talented American footballer and received offers to play at community colleges, however, he decided to pursue an online gaming career.

Dennis has two younger brothers who he sometimes features in his videos.

What is Duke Dennis known for?

The talented gamer launched his first YouTube channel called Dennis Gaming in 2013.

However, it wasn't until 2016 when he started uploading NBA 2K videos he started to gain a lot more subscribers.

He is also one of the founders of the AMP YouTube channel with fellow creators Chrisnxtdoor, Kai Cenat, ImDavisss, Agent 00, and Fanum.

And he has a third channel called DukeDennisLive where he posts live streams and plays Call Of Duty.

Is Duke Dennis on social media?

As well as his three YouTube channels, Dennis also uses Twitter and Instagram where he has two million followers.

He often shares upbeat positive posts about his life with his followers on Instagram.

After the removal of the "blue ticks" on Twitter, Dennis posted to his account: "Don't care how hard life get I'm not buying that blue check" with a laughing emoji.

What is Duke Dennis's net worth?

The successful gamer is worth approximately $4million.

Most of his wealth comes from his YouTube channels through revenue and paid ads.

He also has a Twitch channel with over 450,000 followers and receives donations during streams from his fans.

Dennis has his own clothing brand called DeeBlock, which sells hoodies, T-shirts, and joggers.

Who is Duke Dennis's girlfriend?

It is believed that Dennis is currently dating rapper Kali.
