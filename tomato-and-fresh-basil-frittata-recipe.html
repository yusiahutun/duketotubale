Tomato &amp; Fresh Basil Frittata Recipe

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  192 words  |  Larita Shotwell
Becky Luigart-Stayner

Sliced up and served with potatoes and thick pieces of bacon, this frittata is enjoyed by overnight guests in the dining room of The Cornell Inn.

Yields: 6 serving(s)

Prep Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 45 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe

  • Cooking spray

  • 1/2 pt.

    cherry tomatoes, halved

  • 1/4 c.

    chopped fresh basil

  • 2 oz.

    sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded (about 1/2 cup), divided

  • 2 oz.

    Monterey Jack cheese, shredded (about 1/2 cup), divided

  • 10

    large eggs

  • 1/2 tsp.

    Worcestershire sauce

  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

  • Side salad, bacon, and potatoes, for serving

Directions

  • Step 1Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Lightly grease an 8-by-8-inch baking dish with cooking spray. Combine tomatoes, basil, and 1/3 cup each Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses in the bottom of the baking dish. Whisk together eggs and Worcestershire in a bowl until fully combined and frothy. Season with salt and pepper. Pour over vegetable mixture and sprinkle with remaining cheeses. 
  • Step 2Bake until just cooked through, 25 to 28 minutes. Cut into squares and serve warm with a side salad, bacon, and potatoes.
