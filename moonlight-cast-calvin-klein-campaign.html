Cast of Moonlight Stars in Calvin Klein Underwear Campaign

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  240 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Hot off the heels of their huge Oscars win for Best Picture last night, the cast of Moonlight has something else to celebrate this morning: a new campaign for Calvin Klein Underwear.

The film's stars Mahershala Ali (who became the first Muslim actor to win an Oscar last night), Ashton Sanders, Trevante Rhodes and Alex Hibbert all landed a spot in Calvin Klein's new black and white ads for Spring 2017. The striking photos, all captured by Willy Vanderperre, mark the first major fashion campaign for all four actors.

In a statement from the brand, creative director Raf Simons said, "It's an acknowledgement of remarkable actors who are revealing something important of being a man today in what they do."
