Career

Zainab moved to Los Angeles with the dream of becoming an actress. However, she struggled to make pathways in the industry. Then, one day, an opportunity came calling after she was asked to fill in for a comedy show producer. At first, she did not want the gig but figured it was decent paying and would only be for a few days.

She found the gig exciting, which led her to develop a passion for comedy. As she interacted with comedians, she watched them, seeing what made their jokes work and what didn't. She also noticed that the industry was heavily dominated by male talent.

After six months, Zainab quit working as an assistant. She looked up open mic events and attended one close to her home. The first performance was excellent, with the crowd responding positively. However, she thought it might have been a fluke, leading her to attend another event to be sure.
