The Best Shorts for Women Over 50: Our Favorite Pairs

 Posted on February 1, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1145 words  |  Larita Shotwell

God bless women who continue to wear shorts with supreme confidence regardless of their age. You look great! As for the rest of us (because it can’t be only me), the thought of sporting a pair of shorts started to lose its appeal after the age of about 40. That’s when I slowly started filling my closet with flowy skirts and sundresses and shoving the shorts to the back of the drawer.

Sometimes, though, you really need to pull on something easy, cool, versatile and casual. In other words, you need a pair of shorts! Whether you’re riding a bike, sitting on the grass at a picnic, on a vacation, or roughhousing with grandchildren, shorts offer a level of comfort and practicality that dresses and skirts can’t touch.

With that in mind, we’ve rounded up seven pairs of shorts that are easy to wear at any age. Whether you want to dress up or dress down, spend a little or a lot, downplay your derriere or camouflage dimpled thighs, one of these will work for you. Finding shorts for women over 50 can be tricky, but when in doubt, go classic. Available exclusively online, these chino shorts come in a rainbow of delicious hues, including citrus (pictured), cantaloupe, and olive. A hint of stretch in the soft cotton fabric makes them perfect for active days, and a high rise means you won’t be tugging at them or fussing with the fit. Pockets lend practicality, and the fact that they’re machine-washable make these shorts a perfect choice for everyday wear.
