Jameela Jamil Just Dyed Her Hair Bright Orange

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  244 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Jameela Jamil just debuted a new hair color and it is straight fire (like, literally). On Sept. 30, the Good Place star took to Instagram to share her spicy new look that also happens to be the "it" color of the season: bright orange.

"Ok boomer," she captioned the photo that captures her flashing a soft smile and a wink at the camera. Her signature fringe was slightly shorter and cut straight across, while the rest of her brightly-hued hair was styled in waves. She complimented the color with a thick black cat eye and a pinkish-nude lip. IMO her new color slightly resembles a pumpkin spice latte — in the best way possible.

And the fiery color is surely on the fast track to becoming the biggest autumnal hair trend, and Jamil is just the latest celebrity to hop on board. She's in great, fashionable company, joining Sophie Turner and Gigi Hadid, who recently both went orange-red tone ahead of the season. Another actress to go for the bold color (or go back to the color, I should say) is Modern Family alum Ariel Winter.

Other celebrities, like Dua Lipa and Ashley Benson, also recently gave the eye-catching color a test drive, with the former repping carrot-colored hair in a Versace campaign.

I don't know if it's inspired by our pumpkin-spice loving basic tendencies or the current Twilight renaissance (hello, Victoria!) but expect to see this color everywhere this fall.
