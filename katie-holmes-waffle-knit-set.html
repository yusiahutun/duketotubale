Katie Holmes Just Stepped Out in the Coziest Waffle Knit Set

 Posted on March 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  319 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Katie Holmes has set the blueprint for comfortable, chic dressing this fall.

While out and about in New York City yesterday, the Dawson's Creek alum cut a stylish figure while wearing an oatmeal-colored waffle knit set from Aerie, composed of a collared quarter-zip sweater and roomy drawstring pants. (You can buy the two-piece set now, although the trousers are currently available in only the stone-gray color.)

For extra warmth, she layered her sweater over a brown-and-black flannel button-up. She finished the look with warm caramel accents, including suede mules and a shearling-lined tote bag by Saint Laurent, the latter of which she carried a bouquet of autumnal-themed flowers in.

So far, Holmes has been having a fashion-filled season.

In September, she made a jaw-dropping appearance at Tom Ford's spring 2023 ready-to-wear fashion show in a clingy long-sleeved hooded dress. Then, she accessorized with strappy bronze heeled sandals, a plain black clutch, and an array of sparkling rings.

And while at Bulgari's anniversary party in October, the actress looked gorgeous in a classic black slipdress, which featured a deep V-cut and a ruffled asymmetrical hem. She wore the piece with a golden cuff bracelet, a pendant necklace, and black pointed boots.

Holmes previously opened up about her off-duty style in an interview with BAZAAR.com. "I love my T-shirts, and I love my vintage T-shirts. They're so yummy. They're a little dirty, a little stained or whatever, and that's me," she said at the time.
