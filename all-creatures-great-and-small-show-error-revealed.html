Did you spot this intriguing prop detail in All Creatures Great and Small's new series?

 Posted on March 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  441 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Fans are loving series three of the Channel 5 show

    All Creatures Great and Small returned for a new series recently and fans are overjoyed to have the Channel 5 show back on screens. But in episode one of series three, there was an intriguing detail that eagle-eyed fans may have spotted!

    MORE: Viewers are saying the same thing after All Creatures Great and Small return

    In a scene at the beginning of the episode, which featured Nicholas Ralph in his role as James Herriot, the on-screen vet could be seen drinking from a beer tankard prop that seemingly had no bottom. When the actor lifted the cup to take a 'drink' his face could be seen through the cup.

    WATCH: All Creatures Great and Small series three - official trailer

    One person spotted the prop detail on Twitter and wondered if it was an error. They wrote: "Anyone else notice the bottomless fake beer tankard in #allcreaturesgreatandsmall?" 

    However, despite the detail looking like an error, it's possible that the prop was made that way deliberately as many tankards made in the 1930s were designed with glass bottoms.

    Meanwhile, viewers overall are thrilled about the heartwarming drama's return and have been sharing their praise on social media. A fan tweeted: "Only just watched the new episode of #AllCreaturesGreatAndSmall #ACGAS. It's so nice for it to be back. My favourite all-time program - nothing can beat it."
