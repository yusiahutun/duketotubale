Bio: Height, Weight

Josh Norman - real name Joshua Ricardo Norman- was born on 15 December 1987 in Greenwood, South California. He was raised in a small Baptist church where his father, Roy Norman, worked as a pastor. His mother, Sandra, worked as a nurse and took care of five children, including Josh.

At the age of 11, his parents divorced, and Josh started living with his dad.

He studied at Greenwood High School and played football as a cornerback. Slowly, he gained notoriety from his brutal physical presence in the defense line.
